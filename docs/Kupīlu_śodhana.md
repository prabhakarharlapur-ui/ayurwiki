## Śodhana Composition
| Kupīlu | [Strychnos nux-vomica](Strychnos_nux-vomica.md) | Sd. | 1 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Gomūtra | Cow urine |  | QS |
| Godugdha | Cow milk |  | QS |

## Method
Immmerse Kupīlu seeds in Gomūtra for 7 days, replacing fresh Gomūtra every day. Thereafter, remove, wash with water and do [svedana](svedana.md) in dolā yantra for 4 hours in Godugdha. Remove the testa and embryo, Roast the cotyledon in ghee and pound to prepare powder.
