## Method
Vimala powder is bundled in cloth and same is subjected to [svedana](svedana.md) for three hours in a dolāyantra containing the juice of any one of the ingradients No.2. the Vimala powder should be roasted in an iron pan on strong fire till the excess of sulphur is disappeared. The śodhita Vimala is used for the māraṇā process.
