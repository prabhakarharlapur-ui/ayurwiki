# Sheaths

[TOC]

There is divine spark in us called- 'Atma', the soul, and the five concentric layers of matter enveloping the Atma are called **Sheaths** or **Koshas**.

## Five Sheaths
1. The food sheath - Annamaya Kosha
1. The vital air sheath - Pranamaya Kosha
1. The mental sheath - Manomaya Kosha
1. The intellectual sheath - Vijnamaya Kosha
1. The bliss sheath - Anandamaya Kosha
