## Śodhana Composition
| Jaipāla |  | Sd. | 1 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dugdha | Cow milk | - | QS |
| Nimbū-nīra | - | Fr. | QS |

## Method
The testa of Jaipāla is removed and the seeda are subjected to [svedana](svedana.md) in dolā yantra with godugdha for three hours. The seed is removed. The cotyledons dried and powdered. Bhāvanā with nimbu svarasa is given for three days, and then it is dried in sun and used.
