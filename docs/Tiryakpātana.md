## Tiryakpātana Composition
| Pārada | - | - | 3 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tāmra | - | Lf. | 1 part |
| Nimbū-rasa | - | Fr. | QS |

## Method
Mercury obtained from adhah pātana saṃskāra is amalgated with copper and triturated in lime juice to form a soft paste and mercury is collected through Tiryakpātana yantra.
