# Shastra karma

**Shastra Karma** (*Surgical Interventions*) refers to the Main Surgical procedures as per Ayurveda. The Surgical procedures which can be carried out by any sharp instruments is called Shastra Karma. As per **Sushruta Samhita**, they are 8 in numbers so they ae also called **Ashta Vidha Shastra Karma**. *Chedan, Bhedan, Lekhan, Vedhan, Eshan, Ahran, Vistravan, Seevan* are their names. The Author of **Ashtang Hrudayam** mentioned 13 types of Shastra Karma by Adding Utthpatan, Kuttan, Manthana, Grahan, Dahana.

There is Detailed **Yogya** (Experimental models) to learn all these procedures, mentioned in compendia of Surgery. each procedures has clear indications, contra indications, complications and the texts also includes the pre-operative **Purva Karma**, peri-operative **Pradhana Karma** and post operative **Paschata Karma** management of patient including the management of complications **Updrava**. there are 20 types of Sharp instruments Shastra detailed in texts.
