# Yama

**Yama** is the god of death. yama is also the first of the eight limbs or means of attaining yoga. yamas are universal moral connandments or ethical disciplanes transcending creeds, countries, age and tie. The five mentioned by Patanjali are non voilence, truth, non-staling, continence and non-coveting.
