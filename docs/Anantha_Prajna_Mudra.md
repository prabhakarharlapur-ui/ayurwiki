# Anantha Prajna Mudra

[TOC]

Space, the akasha is infinite. We can develop infinite consciousness with **Ananta Prajna mudra**.
## Formation
Make hooks by touching tips of the middle fingers and thumbs as shown in the picture. Touch the tips of the index fingers. Interlock the remaining fingers so that they touch the back of the palms.

## Effects
Akasha and agni touch each other with crossed fingers. hence the power of [agni](agni.md) - the conciousness, increases infinitely. When vayu - the index fingers, are joined, thinking becomes unbounded. Thoughts travel far and wide with the help of agni and akasha. Awareness becomes infinite and helps to reach others through 'Telepathy'.

## Benefits
* Ananta means unending, infinite.
1. Rishis of India gained infinite awareness by  practising this mudra.
1. This mudra helps to treat people by sending energy vibrations through telepathy. Rishis communicated using this mudra.
