# Kaṅda

**Kaṅda** is a bulbous root, a knot. the Kaṅda is of a round shape os about four inches situated twelve inches above the anus and near the navel, where three main Naadis, Susumna, Ida and Pingala - unite and separate. it is covered as if with a soft white piece of cloth.
