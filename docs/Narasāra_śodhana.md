## Śodhana Composition
| Narasāra |  | Sd. | 1 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Jala |  | - | 3 parts |

## Method
3 times water is added to Narasāra and a solution is made. this is to be filtered and boiled till water is evaporated.
