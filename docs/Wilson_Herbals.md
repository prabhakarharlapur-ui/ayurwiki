# Wilson Herbals

![*Wilson Herbals logo*](images/Wilson_Herbals_logo.jpeg)

Established in **1986**, **Wilson Herbals** is a reputed name in the domain of medical products owing to an wide range of solutions that come with a guarantee of pure natural composition and sheer effectiveness. Based in **Jalandhar, Punjab**, they are an eminent **Manufacturer and Supplier** of an entire gamut of medical utility materials, that include **Ayurvedic Herbal products like Tablets and Capsules**.

## Array Of Products
They provide a complete set of medical solutions that share their lineage with the Ayurveda system and consists of products that include Ayurvedic Herbal products like Tablets and Capsules. The gamut of products offered by them, are as follows:

* Ayurvedic Brain Syrups
* Ayurvedic Cough Syrups
* Ayurvedic Digestive Syrups
* Ayurvedic Herbal Capsules
* Ayurvedic Herbal Tablets
* Herbal Balm
* Herbal Oil for Rheumatic Pain and Muscle Stiffness
* Herbal Tea
