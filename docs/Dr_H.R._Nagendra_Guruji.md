# Dr H.R. Nagendra Guruji

Dr H.R. Nagendra Guruji is an indian mechanical engineer, yoga therapist, Academic writer and the founder vice chanceller of swamy vivekananda anusandhana samsthana (S-vyasa) and of arogyadhama - yoga and ayurveda hospital which is working with holistic approach.
