## Śodhana Composition
| Sudhā dugdha (snuhī) | - | Pl. | 96 g |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Ciñca dala drava | - | Lf. | 24 g |

## Method
Ciñca patra [svarasa](svarasa.md) is mixed with dugdha of snuhī and dried under the sun.
