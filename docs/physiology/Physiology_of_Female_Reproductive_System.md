## General Description
Menstrual blood (‘Raja’) also is a derivative of ‘Rasa’ only. Menstruation occurs in females once in every month and lasts for about three days. This process of menstrual cycle begins at the age of twelve years and stops at about fifty years of age (Su.Sū. 14/6). During the act of copulation, production of ‘Śukra’ occurs in females also; and it should not be thought that it
is of no use in the process of production of embryo i.e., it is also of definite use.
