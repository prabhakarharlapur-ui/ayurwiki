# Yoga sūtra

**Yoga sūtra** is the cassical work on Yoga by Patanjali. It consists of 185 terse aphorisms on yoga and it is divided into four parts dealing respectively with Samadhi, the means by which yoa is attained , the powers the seeker comes across in his quest and the state of absolution.
