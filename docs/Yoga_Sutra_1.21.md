# Yoga Sutra 1.21

> तीव्रसंवेगानामासन्नः ॥२१॥
>
> tīvra-saṁvegānām-āsannaḥ ॥21॥

1. tīvra: *high intensity, very intense*
1. saṁvega: *urge, conviction, strong will*
1. āsannaḥ: *very close, near*

:**Those who have an intense urge attain asamprajnata samadhi very soon.**
:::::OR
:**Samadhi is nearest to those whose desire (for Samhadhi) is intensely strong and those are intensely energetic.**

**Intensity and rate of practice** - The practitioner who moves forward with great level of intensity, at a very fast pace, achieves samadhi faster than the others. Two things are emphasized here; one is pace, the speed at which one is going forward, and second, the conviction, intensity of effort one puts into the practice.
