# Anutaila

[TOC]

Applying 2 drops of **Anutaila** to the nose cures many diseases of Head, Cough, Nose and Mouth.

## List of Ayurvedic herb in which used in this preparation
[Asparagus racemosus](Asparagus_racemosus.md), [Hemidesmus indicus](Hemidesmus_indicus.md)
