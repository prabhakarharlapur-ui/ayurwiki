# Datri Taila

[TOC]

**Datri Taila** acts as a laxative and It is used for stomach ache. Useful to apply on swollen areas and Joints.

## List of Ayurvedic herb in which used in this preparation
[Allium sativum](Allium_sativum.md), [Ricinus communis](Ricinus_communis.md)
