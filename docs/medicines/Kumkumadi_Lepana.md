# Kumkumadi Lepana

Kumkumadi lepana is an age old ayurveda remedy for acne and pimples( known as youvana pitikas in ayurveda), dark circles, pigments, scars and stretch marks. Kumkumadi lepam contains skin friendly herbs like Saffron, sandal wood and Turmeric.

Kumkumadi lepana helps to reduce acne and pimples which erupt due to dry skin. Kumkumadi lepana has to be applied on affected area after cleansing the skin .Neem tulsi soap cleanses the skin perfectly by removing dirt and acts as natural antibiotic too. Saffron in kumkumadi lepana is known for its healing properties and also it imparts glow to skin. Thus saffron heals acne, while turmeric fights with germs and sandal wood soothes the skin which is irritated due to microbial attack.
