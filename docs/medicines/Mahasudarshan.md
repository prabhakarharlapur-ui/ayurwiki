# Mahasudarshan

[TOC]

![*Mahasudarshan*](../images/Mahasudarshanchurna.png)
**Ideal formulation for chronic fever**

1. It is effective in all type of fevers.
1. It has cooling, digestive, bitter-tonic, refrigerant and anthelmintic action
1. It is effective in Malarial fever.
1. It stimulates secretion of digestive enzymes and gastric juices

## Indications
1. All types of fever
1. Post febrile dyspepsia
1. Febrile constipation
1. Quinine induced Tinnitus

## Dose
1 tsf 2 times

## Ingredients
1. Swertia chirayita
1. Azadirachta indica
1. Cyperus rotundus
1. Tinospora cordifolia
1. Picrorhiza kurroa
1. Fumaria  vaillantii, etc.
