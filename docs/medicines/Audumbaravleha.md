# Audumbaravleha

[TOC]

![*Audumbaravleha*](../images/Audumbaravleha.png)

The main active ingredient of this formulation is Ficus glomerata. It has astringent, anti-inflammatory effect on gastric mucosa. It in healing of Peptic ulcer.

## Indications
1. Gastritis
1. Peptic Ulcer
1. Burning Sensation in body.

## Dose
1-2 teaspoonful 2 times.

## Ingredients
1. Ficus glomerata
1. Cuminum cyminum
1. Rubia cordifolia
1. Smilax china
1. Caesalpinia digyna
1. [Hemidesmus indicus](Hemidesmus_indicus.md)
1. Eletteria cardamomum and Sugar.
