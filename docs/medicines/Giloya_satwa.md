## Importance
Giloya satwa can be used in chronic fever, gouts, vomiting, cardiac debility, skin diseases, anaemia, cough, jaundice, seminal weakness, leprosy.

## Dosage
250 mg twice in a day or as directed by physician

## Indications
1. Fever
1. cough
1. Skin disorders
1. low platelet count
1. Dengue
1. Malaria
1. Improve immunity system
1. Effective in fever
1. Helpful in gout
