# Amalaki Rasayana

[TOC]

![*AMALKI RASAYAN*](../images/Small_299822408.png)

## Importance
Baidyanath [Amalki](Amalki.md) Rasayan assists natural internal cleansing & helps reduce acidity. Supports digestive system without aggravating pitta. Nourishes & rejuvenates the tissues. Helps in detoxification of the blood.

## Dosage
1 to 2 teaspoonfuls (5 to 10gm) in the morning and evening with warm milk or as directed by physician.

## Indications
Hyperacidity Indigestion
