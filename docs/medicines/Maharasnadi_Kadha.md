# Maharasnadi Kadha

[TOC]

1. Reduces pain and inflammation
1. Improves functional efficiency of affected part
1. Strength nerves
1. Reduces stiffness of joints
1. Improves appetite
1. Safe, even if taken for long duration

## Indications
Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid arthritis, Sciatica, Low back pain, Stroke, Paraplegia, Degenerative changes of body.

## Dosage
4 teaspoonfuls 2 to 3 times per day with equal quantity of warm water
