# Dārvi Malahara

[TOC]

**Dārvi Malahara** is a semisolid preparation made with the ingredients given in the Formulation composition.

## Method of preparation
* Rasājñana is the dried aqueous extract of the roots of [Dāruharidrā](Dāruharidrā.md), (Berberis aristata or B. asiatica or B. lycium, Fam. Berberidaceae), and is prepared by the following method.
* Chop [Dāruharidrā](Dāruharidrā.md) into small pieces of about 1 cm thickness. Powder the chopped roots to a yavkuta (powder whose all particles pass through sieve number 22 and not more than 10 percent pass through sieve number 44).
* Weigh the powder and transfer to a suitable extraction vessel.

## Description
* Yellowish-brown, non-gritty, smooth gel.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed amber coloured containers, protected from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Sveta Pradara (Leucorrhoea), Yonikandū (Itching), Yoni sotha, (Vaginitis and other wounds and ulcers).

## Dose
* 2g twice a day to be applied with applicator in vagina.

## Anupāna
* Water.
