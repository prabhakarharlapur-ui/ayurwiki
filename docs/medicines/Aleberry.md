# Aleberry

**Aleberry** is a [Beverage](../traditions/Beverage.md) made by boiling ale with spice (such as nutmeg), sugar and bread-sops, the last commonly toasted. It is sweetened, strained, and drunk hot.

The word is "a corruption of ale-bree ... bree (Anglo-Saxon brin, broth).

Aleberry was often used as a domestic remedy for a cold.
