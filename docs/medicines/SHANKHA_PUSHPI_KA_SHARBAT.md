# SHANKHA PUSHPI KA SHARBAT

[TOC]

![*SHANKHA PUSHPI KA SHARBAT*](../images/Small_457289018.jpg)

## Importance
Baidyanath Shankh Pushpi is an effective tonic for higher mental functioning, mental quotient, concentration and learning. Shankh pushpi is an ideal tonic for students preparing for examinations as it keeps the mind healthy and provides relief from headache, anxiety, fatigue, distractibility etc. It helps in relieving mental fatigue.

## Dosage
2 to 4 teaspoon (10 to 20 ml) twice a day with water or as directed by the physician.

## Indications
1. Increase Concentration
1. Increase learning ability
1. Memory booster brain tonic
1. Higher mental functioning
1. Increase Grasping power
