# Suvarnasootshekhar Ras

[TOC]

It is especially useful in hyperacidity associated with nausea, vomiting, epigastric pain, giddiness, hiccups and insomnia. It regulates gastric acid secretion and helps in healing of ulcers. It is also useful in hyperacidity caused due to mental stress. By its cholagogue action it helps increases secretion of bile into the duodenum which is necessary for neutralizing gastric secretion.

## Indications
1. Hyperacidity
1. Gastritis
1. Vomiting
1. Giddiness
1. Headache.

## Dose
1 tab 2 times

## Ingredients
1. Purified Mercury,
1. Purified Sulfur,
1. Aconitum ferox,
1. Suvarna Bhasma,
1. Tamra bhasma,
1. Trikatu (Piper longum, Piper nigrum & Zingiber officinale),
1. Datura metle seeds,
1. Cinnamomum zeylanicum,
1. Cinnamomum tamala,
1. Mesua ferra,
1. Eletteria cardamomum etc.
