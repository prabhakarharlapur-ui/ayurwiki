## General Descripition
Medicines which are used in the form of smoke by burning of it called Dhūpa.

## Method of preparation
The drugs are made in the form of coarse powder and put of the fire to produce smoke.

## Characteristics
* Dhūpa made of plant drugs. The powder should not lose their oroginal colour and smell.

## Preservation
* Dhūpa made of plant drugs. When kept in air tight container can be used for 2 years.
