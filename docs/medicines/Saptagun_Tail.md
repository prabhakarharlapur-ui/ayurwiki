# Saptagun Tail

[TOC]

![*SAPTAGUN TEL*](../images/Small_2016628828.png)

## Importance
Effective in fire burns, wounds, ear pain.

## Dosage
Apply few drops of saptagun tel on infected parts of the body.

## Indications
1. Fire burns
1. Wounds
1. Ear pain
1. Burning sensation
