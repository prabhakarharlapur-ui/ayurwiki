# Cognium Tablet

![*Cognium Tablet*](../images/Cognium-Tablets-195x215.jpg)

**COGNIUM** tablets and syrup are aimed at preserving and enhancing cognitive functions. **COGNIUM** slows down the process of degeneration of neurons through antioxidants, nervine tonics and by improving healthy circulation. **Jala Brahmi** (Bacopa monnieri) and **Jyotishmati** (Celastrus paniculatus) present in COGNIUM improve cognitive functions. **[Brahmi](Brahmi.md)** (Centella asiatica) and **Arjuna** (Terminalia arjuna) enhance circulation to the brain. Stress is one of the brain’s worst enemies. Over time, if left unchecked, chronic stress destroys brain cells and its functions. **[Ashwagandha](Ashwagandha.md)** (Withania somnifera) provides protection to the nervous system by acting as adaptogenic against stress.
