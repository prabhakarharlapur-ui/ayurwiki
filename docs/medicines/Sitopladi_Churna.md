# Sitopladi Churna

[TOC]

![*SITOPLADI CHURNA*](../images/Small_1355089835.png)

## Importance
Sitopladi churna is powder mix of ayurvedic herbs which is very effective in seasonal cough, cold, Asthma and respiratory diseases. Sitopaladi Churna is quite safe for kids. It should be always given after consultation with doctor.

## Dosage
½ - 1 teaspoons (3 – 5 gm) thrice in a day with Baidyanath Honey or as per advised by doctor.

## Indications
1. Effective in Cough and Cold
1. Effective in respiratory Diseases
