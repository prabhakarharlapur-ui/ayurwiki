# Mahaalakshmivilaasa rasa

[TOC]

**Mahaalakshmivilaasa rasa** is used for Influenza, Pneumonia, Asthma, Cough, Fever, Skin diseases, Diabetes, Fistula, Dysentery and Sexual apathy
