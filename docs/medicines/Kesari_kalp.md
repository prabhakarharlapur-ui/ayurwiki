# Kesari kalp

[TOC]

![*KESARI KALP*](../images/Small_1858635346.jpg)

## Importance
Kesari Kalp, A royal chyawanprash is a complex formulation enriched with high content of natural vitamin C, Kesar, Swarn Bhasm, Silver vark and other rare ingredients. It provides all the goodness of Baidyanth Chyawanprash special along with the enriched properties of various unique ingredients.
These exclusive ingredients also make it an excellent remedy to remove symptoms of premature ageing like wrinkles, greying of hair, weight loss, dark circle around eyes, debility and loss of vigour & vitality. Hence it is an excellent formulation for retaining youth made possible due to presence of kesar, giving the product its name Kesari Kalp

## Dosage
1 to 2 teaspoonfuls preferably with milk twice daily.

## Indications
It helps to build up body resistance.
Prevent cough and cold.
Reduces fatigue.
Removes physical and mental tiredness
