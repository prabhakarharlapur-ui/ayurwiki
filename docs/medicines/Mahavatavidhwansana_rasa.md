# Mahavatavidhwansana rasa

[TOC]

* Useful in severe pain of sciatica
* Effective in alleviating pain
* It is useful in acute musculo-skeletal and nervous disorders, Rheumatoid arthritis and Osteo-arthritis.
* It is effective in headache, backache and pain in ribscage

## Indications
1. Sciatica
1. Rheumatoid arthritis
1. Osteoarthritis
1. Headache
1. Pleuritic pain
1. vata disorders.

## Dose
1 to 2 tablets 2 - 3 times

## Ingredients
1. Purified Mercury
1. Purified Sulfur
1. Abhrak bhasma
1. Kansya Bhasma
1. Suvarnamakshik bhasma
1. Hartal bhasma etc
