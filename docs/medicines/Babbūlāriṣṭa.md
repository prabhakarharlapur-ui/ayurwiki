# Babbūlāriṣṭa

[TOC]

**Babbūlāriṣṭa** is a fermented liquid preparation made with the ingredients in the Formulation composition given below. It contains not more than 10 per cent, and not less than 5 per cent of alcohol that is self generated in the preparation over a period of time.

## Method of preparation
* Wash, dry and powder the ingredient numbered 1 ([Kvātha Dravya](Kvātha_Dravya.md)) of the formulation composition and pass through the sieve number 44 to obtain coarse powder.
* Clean, dry and powder the ingredients numbered 5 to 13 ([Prakṣepa Dravya](Prakṣepa_Dravya.md)) of the formulation composition individually and pass through the sieve number 85 to obtain fine powder.
* Add specified amounts of water to the [Kvātha Dravya](Kvātha_Dravya.md), soak overnight, heat, reduce to one fourth and filter through muslin cloth to obtain Kvatha

## Description
* Clear, dark brown liquid without frothing and significant sedimentation; with astringent taste.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed amber coloured bottle, protect from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Kṣaya (Pthisis), Kuṣṭha (Diseases of skin), Atisāra (Diarrhoea), Prameha (Urinary disorder), Śvāsa (Dyspnoea/Asthma), Kāsa (Cough).

## Dose
* 15 – 30 ml orally with equal amount of water after meals twice a day.

## Anupāna
* Water.
