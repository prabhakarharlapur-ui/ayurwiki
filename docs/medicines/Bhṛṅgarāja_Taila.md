# Bhṛṅgarāja Taila

[TOC]

**Bhṛṅgarāja Taila** is a medicated oil preparation made with the ingredients in the Formulation Composition given below with [mūrcchita](mūrcchita.md) Tila taila as the basic ingredient.

## Method of preparation
* Take all ingredients of pharmacopoeial quality.
* Treat Tila taila to prepare [mūrcchita](mūrcchita.md) Tila taila.
* Treat Gairika to prepare Gairika - Śuddha.
* Take fresh Bhṛṅgarāja and wash thoroughly with water. Grind and filter through muslin cloth to obtain [svarasa](svarasa.md).
* Wash, clean, dry the ingredients numbered 3 to 6 and 8 to 14 of the Formulation Composition,
powder separately and pass through 180 µm I. S. sieve (sieve number 85) ([Kalka](../Kalka.md) dravya).

## Description
* A medicated oil, light brownish yellow in color with a pleasant odour.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed containers, protected from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Keśapāta (Loss of hair), Netraroga (Diseases of eyes), Karṇaroga (Diseases of ear), Śiroroga (Diseases of head), Manyastambha (Neck rigidity/Torticollis), Galagraha (Difficulty in swallowing), Khālitya (Premature baldness), Indralupta (Alopecia areata).

## Dose
* External application over the scalp for shiro-Abhyanga.
