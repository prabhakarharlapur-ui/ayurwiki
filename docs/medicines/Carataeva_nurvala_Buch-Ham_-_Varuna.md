# Carataeva nurvala Buch-Ham - Varuna

[TOC]

**Varuna** consists of dried stem bark of Crataeva nurvala Buch-Ham (Fam.Capparidaceae), a small wild or cultivated tree found throughout the year in India, often
found along streams, also in dry, deep boulder formation in Sub-Himalayan tracts.
