# Balāriṣṭa

[TOC]

**Balāriṣṭa** is a fermented liquid preparation, made with the ingredients in the Formulation composition given below. It contains not more than 10 per cent, and not less than 5 per cent of alcohol that is self generated in the preparation over a period of time.

## Method of preparation
* Wash, dry and powder the ingredients numbered 1 and 2 ([Kvātha Dravya](Kvātha_Dravya.md)) of the formulation composition.
* Clean, dry and powder the ingredients numbered 6 to 13 ([Prakṣepa Dravya](Prakṣepa_Dravya.md)) of the formulation composition individually and pass through the sieve number 85 to obtain fine powder.
* Add specified amount of water to the [Kvātha](../Kvātha.md) Dravya, soak overnight, heat, reduce to one fourth and filter through muslin cloth to obtain [Kvātha](../Kvātha.md)

## Description
* Clear brown liquid without frothing and significant sedimentation; with aromatic odour and sweet taste.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed amber coloured bottle, protect from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Agnimāndya (Digestive impairment), Daurbalya (Weakness), Vātaja Roga (Diseases due to vata doṣa), Kārśya (Emaciation).

## Dose
* 15 – 30 ml orally with equal amount of water after meals twice a day.

## Anupāna
* Water.
