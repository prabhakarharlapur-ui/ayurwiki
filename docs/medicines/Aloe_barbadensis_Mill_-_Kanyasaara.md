# Aloe barbadensis Mill - Kanyasaara

[TOC]

**Kanyasaara** consists of dried juice of leaves of Aloe barbadensis Mill. Syn. Aloe vera Tourn.ex Linn, Aloe indica Royle. (Fam. Liliaceae), shrub planted in many Indian gardens and found growing throughout India.
