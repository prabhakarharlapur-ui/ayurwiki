# Elettaria cardamo0mum (Linn.) R.Br - Sooksmialaa

[TOC]

Sooksmialaa consists seeds of dried fruits of Elettaria cardamomum (Linn.) Maton and its varieties (Fam. Zingiberaceae), a stout large perennial herb, growing naturally in
moist forests of western ghats up to 1500 m, also cultivated in many other parts of south India at an elevation from 750-1500m.
