# Shrigopal Tail

![*Dabur Shrigopal Tail*](../../images/60-shrigopal.jpg)

**Dabur Shrigopal Tail**, is a powerful combination of 61 unique herbs including Ashwagandha, Kesar and Shatavari that help men regain their lost sexual strength. It is a classical remedy for lack of sexual desire, loss of penile tension, erectile dysfunction and other sex related problems. With its regular use, you will experience a boost in sexual strength and stamina that will greatly increase sexual pleasure.
