# Mahayogaraj Guggulu

[TOC]

![*MAHAYOGARAJ GUGGULU*](../../images/Small_717042847.jpg)

## Importance
It is widely used in the Ayurvedic treatment of joint diseases, joint pain, Gout and arthritis

## Dosage
1 tablet twice in a day after food or as directed by physician.

## Indications
1. Effective in Hemiplegia
1. Rheumatism
1. Paraplegia
