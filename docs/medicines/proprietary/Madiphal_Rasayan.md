# Madiphal Rasayan

[TOC]

It induces salivary and gastro intestinal tract secretions.
It acts as appetizer and digestive.
It has carminative and Cholagogue action.

## Ingredients
Citrus medica, Piper nigrum, Piper longum, Tachyspemum ammi, Plumbago zeylanica etc.

## Indications
Anorexia, Indigestion, Mild constipation, vomiting, Liver disorders, abdominal pain, Heart diseare.

## Dose
2 tab 2 times
