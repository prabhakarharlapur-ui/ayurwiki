# Mahasugandit Tail

[TOC]

![*MAHASUGANDHIT TAIL*](../../images/Small_562065979.jpg)

## Importance
Very effective Ayurvedic oil for tension, metal excitement, migraine and headache. It is made with cool Neelgiri and kapoor which are very effective for headache and migraine. As per the physician a good massage with mahasugandhit oil before sleeping provides good sleep and release tiredness.

## Dosage
Put few drops of Mahasugandhit oil on hand and rub gently on hair and then massage hair softly with the help of hand.

## Indications
1. Migraine
1. cool oil
1. Thanda & fresh
1. Headache
1. Mental tiredness
