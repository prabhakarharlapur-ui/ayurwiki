# Alpitone

[TOC]

![*Alpitone*](../../images/Zandu-alpitone.jpg)

Improves appetite, stimulates digestion. Symptoms are as follows: Anorexia, nutritional deficiencies, general debility, fatigue, stress, convalescence following chronic illness & as a general purpose tonic.

## Composition
Each 5 ml contains- [Ashwagandha](Ashwagandha.md)(Withania somnifera) 200 mg Bala(Sida cordifolia) 200 mg Shatavari(Asparagus racemosus) 100 mg Chitrak(Plumbago zeylanica) 50 mg Gokshur (Tribulus terrestris) 50 mg Rasna (Pluchea lanceolata) 50 mg Draksha (Vitis vinifera) 50 mg Amruta (Tinospora cordifolia) 25 mg Sunthi (Zingiber officinale) 25 mg.

## Dosage
Adults: 1-2 tablespoonful twice a day before meals. Children: 1-2 tablespoon twice a day before meals.

* Rejuvinates the body & improves resistance to change. Rich in natural antioxidants such as Amruta (Tinospora cordifolia),Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera), Draksha (Vitis vinifera) and Sunthi (Zingiber officianale). Stimulates digestion,enhances appetite and promotes healthy growth. Helps to reduce stress and fatigue,improves mental and physical efficiency. Improves digestive system and liver function.
