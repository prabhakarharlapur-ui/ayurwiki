# Trifgol

![*Dabur Trifgol*](../../images/33-trifgol.jpg)

**Dabur Trifgol** is a highly effective colon cleanser that provides digestive support and alleviates constipation. Its blend of natural ingredients including Isabgol and Triphala helps cure intestinal complications.  It improves the functioning of the digestive organs and flushes the harmful chemicals and toxins out.
