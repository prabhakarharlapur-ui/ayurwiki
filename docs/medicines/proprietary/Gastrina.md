# Gastrina

![Gastrina Tablets](../../images/101-gastrinia_(1).jpg)

**Dabur Gastrina** is an Ayurvedic solution to gas problems. Its regular use helps in relieving a multitude of abdominal issues including abdominal gas, abdominal discomfort, belching, feeling of fullness and abdominal pain. The natural ingredients in the tablet include citrus lemon, ferula asafetida, black salt etc., makes it a natural and safe cure for stomach related diseases.
