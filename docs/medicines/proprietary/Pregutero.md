# Pregutero

[TOC]

![*Pregutero*](../../images/Pregutero.png)

Preg-Utero has been formulated with an objective of a complete supplement to pregnant women to take care of the common complaints of pregnancy that are the result of biochemical and emotional disturbances in the expectant mother. During the pregnancy period,  the developing foetus makes exacting demands on the physiology of the expectant mother, with the result that the hormonal and the metabolic balance of the mother gets upset, leading to complaints like morning sickness, flatulence, dyspepsia, anorexia, aches, girdle pains, weakness, anaemia, giddiness, swelling of the legs, toxemia etc.

## Indication
IUGR, bad obstetric history, morning sickness, anemia of pregnancy, general tonic

## Dose
2 - 4 teaspoonful twice a day

## Ingredients
Uraria picta, Cida cordifolia, Prunus, Adhatoda vasica, Centella asiatica, Withania somnifera, Asparagus racemosus, Symplocos racemosa, Glycirrhiza glabra
