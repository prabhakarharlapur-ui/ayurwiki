# Sumenta

![*Sumenta*](../../images/SUMENTA-TABLETS-195x215.jpg)

**SUMENTA** helps improve all psychological activities. It is an excellent antidepressant, anxiolytic drug with mild tranquilizing property, but without any side effects.

SUMENTA is the herbal combination of anxiolytics, antistress, and adaptogenic ingredients. Brahmi (Herpestis monniera) exerts anxiolytic activity and reduces stress & anxiety. Tagara (Valeriana wallichii) and Jatamansi (Nardostachys jatamansi) possess soothing and sedative action on the central nervous system thereby relaxing the nervous system. Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera) acts as a mood stabilizer. All these ingredients are proven to be effective in cases of mixed anxiety depression and associated other disorders.

Thus SUMENTA is recommended as first-line therapy in mild to moderate cases of depression and other psychiatric ailments. Sumenta does not cause drowsiness or sedation and thus can be taken during daytime without any hesitation.
