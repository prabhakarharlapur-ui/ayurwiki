# Madhumehari yog

[TOC]

![*MADHUMEHARI YOG*](../../images/Small_724544194.jpg)

## Importance
Madhumehari yog is ayurvedic treatment of Diabetes. Useful to dryness of throat, relieve frequently urination, and debility associated with diabetes.

## Dosage
2-3 tablets two times a day with water, or as directed by Physician.

## Indications
1. Control Diabetes
1. effective in dryness of throat
1. help in frequent urination
