# Madhumehari Granuals

[TOC]

![*MADHUMEHARI GRANULES*](../../images/Small_1679746922.jpg)

## Importance
Effective in diabetes, Promotes balanced blood sugar levels, Supports proper function of the pancreases, Maintains healthy digestion and maintains insulin level.

## Dosage
5 gm in a day or as directed by the physician.

## Indications
Useful in controlling Diabetes
