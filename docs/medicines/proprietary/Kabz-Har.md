# Kabz-Har

[TOC]

![*KABZ - HAR*](../../images/Small_1058060646.jpg)

## Importance
Useful in Abdominal Discomfort due to Timpanist’s, Constipation, Indigestion, Flatulence, Belching, Anorexia, Loss of Appetite, Abdominal Colic, Intestinal Worms, Hepatic Disorders etc.

## Dosage
5-10g (1-2 Tablespoonfuls) with warm water.

## Indications
1. Constipation
1. Digestive disorders
1. Belching
1. Anorexia
1. Loss of appetit
