# Sandu Gulkand

[TOC]

![*Sandu Gulkand*](../../images/Gulkand.png)

Sandu Gulkand has sweet taste and coolant effect.
It is useful in summer season to provide protection against summer heat.
It is useful in vitiation of pittadosha when body heat increases due metabolic process of body.
It can be safely given to children, pregnant women as well as elderly people.
It neutralizes excess heat as well as sourness of vitiated pittadosha by its coolant & sweet properly.
In summer season when person is exhausted it provides energy & stamina to the person.
By mild laxative action it is useful to relieve constipation ( by providing diet fiber)
It is also indicated in cases of excessive perspiration.

## Indication
Constipation, burning sensation, hyperacidity, burning sensation in eyes, prickly heat, skin tanning

## Dose
2 tsf  2 times.

## Ingredients
Rosa centifolia, Sugar.
