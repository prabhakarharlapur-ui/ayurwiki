# Dant manjan

[TOC]

![*LAL DANT MANJAN*](../../images/Small_311171590.png)

## Importance
Lal dant manjan is used for dental problems. it is antiseptic for teethes. It prevent and cure dental problems like bleeding of gums, dental carries, pyorrhoea. Also keeps the gum tissues in a healthy condition.

## Dosage
Apply on teeth and massage gently with help of finger.

## Indications
1. Strengthens the gums
1. Stop bleeding from gums
1. Remove foul smell of the mouth
1. Make teeth healthy
1. Prevent from Pyorrhoea
