# Dashamoola Katutrayadi Kashayam

**Dashamoola Katutrayadi Kashayam** is an Ayurvedic medicine usually prescribed by Ayurvedic doctors for Cough, bronchitis and allergic conditions. The medicine is a mixture of herbs in liquid in the form of [Kashayam](../medicines/Kashayam.md).
