# ImuDab Syrup

![Imudab Syrup](../images/102-ImuDab-syrup.jpg)

Dabur ImuDab Syrup is a natural way to boost the immunity and strength among children. It is packed with natural anti-oxidants that fight damage caused by free radicals, which are essentially toxic molecules. It helps battle poor immunity, infections and respiratory problems etc.
