# Sukumaara rasayanam

[TOC]

**Sukumaara rasayanam** is used to treat Stomach pain and Stomach related problems. It is used as tonic for pregnant women(Post 7 months of pregnancy). It is Diuretic, Laxative and Useful for relief of Stomach ache and Ulcer inside stomach.

## List of Ayurvedic herb in which used in this preparation
[Trachyspermum ammi](Trachyspermum_ammi.md), [Ricinus communis](Ricinus_communis.md), [Saccharum Officinarum](Saccharum_Officinarum.md)
