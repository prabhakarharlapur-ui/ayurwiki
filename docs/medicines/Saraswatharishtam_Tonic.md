# Saraswatharishtam Tonic

![*Saraswatharishtam Tonic*](../images/Ayurvedic-Brain-Tonic.jpg)

** Saraswatharishtam Tonic** (Ayurvedic Brain Tonic) - Saraswatarishta gold is a anti-aging herbs tonic. It is a good memory tonic. It is good for people of all ages, improves immunity, voice tone and strength. It is also used in mental conditions, and to improve quality of semen and sperms.
