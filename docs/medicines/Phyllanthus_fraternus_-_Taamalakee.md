# Phyllanthus fraternus - Taamalakee

[TOC]

**Taamalakee** consists of root, stem and leaf of Phyllanthus fraternus Webst. Syn. Phyllanthus niruri Hook. f. non Linn. (Fam. Euphorbiaceae), an annual herb, 20-60cm high, found in Central and Southern India extending to Ceylon.
