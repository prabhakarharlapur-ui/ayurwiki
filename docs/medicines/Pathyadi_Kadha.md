# Pathyadi Kadha

[TOC]

![*Pathyadi Kadha*](../images/Pathyadikadha.png)

**Eeffective medicine in Migraine**
1. Helps to reduce frequency of Migraine attacks
1. Reduces intensity of pain in Migraine
1. It reverses the acute vasodilatation of extra-cranial blood vessels and improves their structural and functional integrity.
1. It has anti-oxidant and adaptogenic properties
1. Has laxative action that relieves constipation
1. Has beneficial effect on eyes

## Indication
1. Migraine
1. Constipation induced headaches
1. Headaches due to eyestrain

## Dose
4 tsf 2 times.

## Ingredients
1. Terminalia chebula
1. Terminalia bellerica
1. Emblica officinalis
1. Curcuma longa
1. Tinospora cordifolia
1. Azadirachta indica.
