# Glycyrrhiza glabra Linn - Yashthi

[TOC]

Yashthi consists of dried, unpeeled, stolon and root of Glycyrrhiza glabra Linn, (Fam. Leguminosae) , a tall perennial herb, upto 2 m high found cultivated in Europe.
Persia, Afghanistan and to little extent in some parts of India.

## Chemical Composition
Glycyrrhizin, glycyrrhizic acid, glycyrrhetinic acid, asparagine, sugars, resin and starch
