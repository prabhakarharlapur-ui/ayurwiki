# Cephagraine Nasal Drops

![*Cephagraine Nasal Drops*](../images/Cephagraine-Nasal-Drops-195x215.jpg)

In sinusitis, **CEPHAGRAINE** nasal drops reduce inflammation, liquefy mucus and relieve congestion. CEPHAGRAINE reduces frequency, severity and duration of migraine by its anti-inflammatory and analgesic property. Shunthi (Zingiber officinale) and Erand (Ricinus communis) have analgesic properties and help to relieve the symptoms of migraine.[Yashtimadhu](Yashtimadhu.md) (Glycyrrhiza glabra) has adaptogenic and anxiolytic properties. Karpoor (Camphora officinarum) and Limbu swaras (Citrus limon) relieve nasal congestion and reduce the heaviness in the head.
