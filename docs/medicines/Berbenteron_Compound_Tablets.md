# Berbenteron Compound Tablets

[TOC]

![*Berbenteron Compound Tablets*](../images/Berbenteronecompound.png)

It is useful in gastrointestinal disorders like diarrhoea and dysentery of bacterial as or amoebic origin.
It possess antibacterial, anti-inflammatory, antispasmodic, carminative and astringent action.
It is found to be effective in Irritable Bowel Syndrome.

## Indications
Diarrhoea, Dysentery, Irritable Bowel Syndrome, Flatulence.

## Dose
2 tablets 3-4 times per day.

## Ingredients
1. [Daruharidra](Daruharidra.md) (Berberis aristata)
1. [Girimallika](Girimallika.md) (Holarrhena antidysenterica)
1. Myristica fragrance,
1. Syzygium aromaticum,
1. Punica granatum
1. Quercus infectorius
