# Paṅcāmṛta lauha Guggulu

[TOC]

**Paṅcāmṛta lauha Guggulu** Vati is a brown spherical pill preparation made with the ingredients in the Formulation composition given below with Guggulu as the basic ingredient.

## Method of preparation
* Take all the ingredients of the pharmacopoeial quality.
* Weigh separately the ingredients numbered 2 to 6 of the formulation composition separately and pass through sieve numbered 85 in the required quantities and mix.
* Prepare [Kajjali](Kajjali.md) from Śuddha Pārada and Śuddha Gandhaka.
* Crush weighed quantity of Śuddha Guggulu, add fine powder of other mixed ingredients to it and pound well. Add Katu Taila to an extent required to facilitate the pounding and continue pounding tment), Vataroga (Disease due to Vata Dosha).ill a semisolid uniformly mixed mass of suitable plasticity is obtained.

## Description
* Dark brown spherical pills with pleasant odour, sandy sensation on tongue with no characteristic taste.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed container, protect from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Mastiṣkaroga (Brain disease), Snāyurujā (Pain in liga)

## Dose
* 125 - 250 mg twice a day.

## Anupāna
* Water and milk.
