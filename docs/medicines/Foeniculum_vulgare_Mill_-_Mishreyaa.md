# Foeniculum vulgare Mill - Mishreyaa

[TOC]

**Mishreyaa** consists of dried ripe fruits of Foeniculum vulgare Mill (Fam.Umbelliferae) , an erect, glabrous, aromatic herb, 1-2 m high, cultivated extensively
throughout India upto 1830 m and also sometimes found wild, fruits ripen in September, stems cut with sickles and put up in loose sheaves to dry in sun, when dry, fruits are beaten out in a cloth in sun, cleaned by winnowing and collected.
