## Method of preparation
* Take all ingredients of pharmacopoeial quality.
* Treat Tila taila to prepare [murcchita](murcchita.md) Tila taila.
* Take fresh [Nili](Nili.md), [Bhrngaraja](Bhrngaraja.md), [Kakatikta](Kakatikta.md) and [Aamalaki](Aamalaki.md) and wash thoroughly with water. Grind and filter through muslin cloth to obtain [svarasa](svarasa.md).
* Wash and clean the cut pieces of Narikela, grind and squeeze through muslin cloth to obtain Narikela Ksheeera.
* Strain the [Ajadugdha](Ajadugdha.md), [Mahishidugdha](Mahishidugdha.md) and [Godugdha](Godugdha.md) through muslin cloth.
* Wash, clean, dry the ingredients numbered 10 to 12 ([kalka](kalka.md) dravya) of the Formulation Composition, powder separately and pass through 180 µm I. S. sieve (sieve number 85) to obtain fine powder. Transfer the powdered ingredients to wet grinder and grind with sufficient quantity of water to prepare homogeneous blend ([kalka](kalka.md)).

## Description
* Medicated oil, dark brown in colour, odour characteristic of sesame oil.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed containers, protected from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Kesapata (Hair fall) and Palita (Premature greying of hair).

## Dose
* External application for siro-abhyanga.
