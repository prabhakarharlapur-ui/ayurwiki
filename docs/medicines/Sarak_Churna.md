# Sarak Churna

[TOC]

Sarak Churna has been formulated keeping in view the modern day requirements of a safe and effective product to correct constipation at its root, thereby ensuring the bliss of complete bowel evacuation.

## Indications
Constipation, adjuvant in the treatment of piles and fistula

## Dose
1 Tablet with lukewarm water at bedtime or 1 - 2 teaspoonful churna (powder) with lukewarm water.

## Ingredients
Senna Casia angustifolia), Glycirrhiza glabra, Rosa centifolia, Zinziber officinale, Anethum sowa, Embelia ribes, Tachyspermum ami, Acorus calamus, Embelia ribes
