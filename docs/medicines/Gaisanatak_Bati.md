## Importance
Gaisantak Bati is effective tablet in Acid - Peptic disorders / Dyspepsia, Gaseous Distension of Abdomen, Anorexia, Indigestion, Constipation, Flatulence etc. it also provides relief from gas

## Dosage
1-2 tablet in a day with water after food.

## Indications
1. Remove Acidity
1. Remove digestion disorder
1. Remove Constipation
1. Removes gas
