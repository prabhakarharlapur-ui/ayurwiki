# Panchajeeraka Gudam

[TOC]

**Panchajeeraka Gudam** vigorously helps in dealing with a wide range of gastrointestinal irregularities as well as Constipation, Heartburn, Flatulence, Peptic ulcer, Gerd or gastroesophageal reflux disease and Stomach pain

## List of Ayurvedic herb in which used in this preparation
