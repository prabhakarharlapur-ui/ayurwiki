# Pudin Hara

![*Dabur Pudin Hara*](../images/39-39-pudin-hara.jpg)

**Dabur Pudin Hara** contains Pudina Satva as the main ingredient and is known to give quick relief from stomach ailments like stomach ache, gas and indigestion. A trusted and fast-action remedy, Pudin Hara is 100% natural and safe.
