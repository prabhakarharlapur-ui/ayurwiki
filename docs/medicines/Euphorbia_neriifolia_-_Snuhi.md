# Euphorbia neriifolia - Snuhi

[TOC]

Snuhi consists of stem of Euphorbia neriifolia Linn. (Fam. Euphorbiaceae), a large branched, erect, glabrous, succulent, xerophytic shrub occurring wild on rocky ground
throughout central India and extensively grown as a hedge plant.
