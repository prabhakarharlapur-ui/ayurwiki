## Importance
Issabbel is an ayurvedic formulation in granules form effective in dysentery, loss of appetite, constipation, Diarrhoea. It maintains digestive disorder and abdominal colic also.

## Dosage
5-7 gm twice in a day or as directed by physician.

## Indications
1. Maintain digestive disorder
1. Dysentery
1. Diarrhoea
1. Abdominal Colic
1. Constipation
