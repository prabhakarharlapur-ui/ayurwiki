## Importance
Livrol tablet is ayurvedic formulation useful in Hyperacidity, Dyspepsia, Gastric, Anorexia, Indigestion, Loss of appetite, Hepatitis, Dry / Bleeding Haemorrhoids, Abdominal Colic, Constipation, Dysentery, Mal- Absorption Syndrome, Haemorrhagic Bacillary Diarrhoea, Enlargement of spleen

## Dosage
1-2 tablets twice or thrice in a day with warm water or as directed by physician

## Indications
1. Hyperacidity
1. Dyspepsia
1. Indigestion
1. Loss of appetite
1. Jaundice
1. Abdominal colic
1. Constipation
1. Dysentery
1. Enlargement of spleen
