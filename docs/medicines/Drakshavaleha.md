## Importance
Drakshavaleha is [Ayurvedic medicine](../concepts/Ayurvedic_medicine.md) formulation effective in hyper acidity, constipation, heart burn due to acidity, dyspepsia etc.

## Dosage
1-2 tablespoonful twice in a day or as directed by physician.

## Indications
1. Hyper Acidity
1. Dyspepsia
1. Heart Burn
1. Anorexia
