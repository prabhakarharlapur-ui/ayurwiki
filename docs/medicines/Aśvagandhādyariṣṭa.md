# Aśvagandhādyariṣṭa

[TOC]

## Method of preparation
* Take the raw materials of pharmacopoeial quality.
* Wash, dry and powder the ingredients numbered 1 to 18 ([Kvātha Dravya](Kvātha_Dravya.md)) of the formulation composition individually and pass through the sieve number 44 to obtain coarse powder.
* Clean, dry and powder the ingredients numbered 22 to 29 ([Prakṣepa Dravya](Prakṣepa_Dravya.md)) of the formulation composition individually and pass through the sieve number 85 to obtain fine powder.
* Add specified amounts of water to the [Kvātha Dravya](Kvātha_Dravya.md), soak overnight, heat, reduce to one eighth and filter through muslin cloth to obtain [Kvātha](../Kvātha.md). Allow to cool.

## Description
* Clear, dark brown liquid without frothing and significant sedimentation; with astringent taste.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed amber coloured bottle, protect from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Murccha (Syncope), Apasmara (Epilepsy), shosa (Cachexia), Unmada (Mania/Psychosis), Karshya (Emaciation), Arsha (Piles), Agnimandya (Digestive impairment), Vataroga (Neurological disorders).

## Dose
* 15 – 30 ml orally with equal amount of water after meals twice a day.
