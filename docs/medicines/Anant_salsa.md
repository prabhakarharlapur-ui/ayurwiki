# Anant salsa

[TOC]

![*ANANT SALSA*](../images/Ananta_sala.png)
## Importance
Anant Salsa is [ayurvedic medicine](ayurvedic_medicine.md) used as blood purifier; it is also effective in removing Acne, Boils and pimples. It also helps in toned up skin and gives you glowing skin without any skin disease.

## Dosage
10-20 ml twice in a day or as directed by physician.

## Indications
1. Purify blood
1. Toned up skin
1. Remove Acne
1. Remove Boils and pimples
1. Gives you lustful and glowing skin
