# Nigella sativa Linn - UPAKUNCIKA

[TOC]

**Upakuncika** consists of seeds of Nigella sativa Linn. (Fam. Ranunculaceae), a small herb, 45 -60 cm high, mostly cultivated in Punjab, Himachal Pradesh, Bihar and Assam.

## Chemical Composition
Essential oil, fixed oil, resin, saponin and tannin
