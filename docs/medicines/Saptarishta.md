# Saptarishta

[TOC]

![*SAPTARISHTA*](../images/Small_577288397.png)

## Importance
Saptarishta is an excellent tonic for Sapt Dhatu Poshan. It provides proper nourishment to body’s structural components such as blood, muscle, bones etc, Called as DHATUS in ayurveda. It Improve the working capacity and efficiency of the body, allowing it to perform to its potential. It emphasizes on total body care rather than individual organ care. Saptarishta holistic approach focuses on complete body care, which automatically takes care of all individual organs.

## Dosage
15 to 30 ml after meals with equal quantity of water or as directed b physician.

## Indications
1. Anti Stress
1. Energy provider
1. Immunity Enhancer
1. Tissue Nourishment
1. Maintain Digestion
1. Help in Blood circulation
