# Panchasav

[TOC]

![*PANCHASAV*](../images/Small_1162601265.png)

## Importance
Baidyanath Panchasav is ayurvedic tonic with power of 5 asav, effective digestive syrup which cures all digestive disorder such as gas, constipation, acidity, abdominal colic, abdominal pain, indigestion, enhance immunity system.

## Dosage
15 to 30 ml after meals with equal quantity of water or as directed b physician.

## Indications
1. Maintain Digestive disorder
1. Improve immunity system
1. Anti-inflammatory
1. Iron tonic for Women
1. Effective in liver disease
