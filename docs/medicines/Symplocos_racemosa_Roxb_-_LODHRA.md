# Symplocos racemosa Roxb - LODHRA

[TOC]

**Lodhra** consists of dried stem bark of Symplocos racemosa Roxb. (Fam.Symplocaceae): an evergreen tree, 6-8.5 m tall, found abundantly in plains and lower hills
throughout India.

## Chemical Composition
Alkaloids (loturine and colloturine) and red colouring matter.
