## Method of preparation
* Take all ingredients of pharmacopoeial quality.
* Wash and dry all the raw materials thoroughly.
* Treat [Ghrta](Ghrta.md) to prepare [Murchita Ghrta](Murchita_Ghrta.md).
* Pulverize Gokshura ([kvatha dravya](kvatha_dravya.md)) to coarse powder and add 16 parts of water, heat and reduce the volume to one fourth. Filter with muslin cloth to obtain Gokshura [kvatha](kvatha.md).
* Treat Śilājatu to prepare Śodhita Śilājatu, and keep aside for addition during snehapāka.

## Description
* A low melting Ghrta, greenish in color with pleasant odour and bitter taste.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed containers, protected from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Mūtra krcchra (Dysuria), Prameha (Metabolic disorders), Aśmarī (Urinary calculus), Mūtra śarkarā (Gravels in urine), Mūtra doṣa (Urinary disorders) and Mūtra dāha (Burning micturition).

## Dose
* 12 g daily in divided doses.

## Anupāna
* Warm water, Trna paňca mūla Kvātha, Warm milk.
