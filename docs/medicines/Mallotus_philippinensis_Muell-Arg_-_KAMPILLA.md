# Mallotus philippinensis Muell-Arg - KAMPILLA

[TOC]

**Kampilla** consists of glands and hairs of fruit of Mallotus philippinensis Muell Arg. (Fam. Euphorbiaceae), a very common perennial shrub or small tree found in outer
Himalayas ascending to 1500 m, mature fruits collected in February-March, reddish brown powder collected in cloth by shaking and rubbing the fruits with hands.
