# Gossypium herbaceum - Kaarpaasa

[TOC]

**Kaarpaasa** consists of seeds (devoid of lint) of Gossypium herbaceum Linn. (Fam.Malvaceae) , an annual or perennial shrub, 0.6-2.4m high, extensively cultivated in India.
