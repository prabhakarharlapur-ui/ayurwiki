# Rasaraj Ras

[TOC]

**It is an excellent Vata pacifying, rejuvenative, brain tonic, heart tonic and spermatogenic. It has benefecial effects on brain, heart and nerves.**

1. Useful in stroke associated with high blood pressure.
1. Corrects function of heart, brain and lungs.
1. Strengthens nerves and improves blood circulation.
1. Corrects the function of kidneys.
1. Strengthens male reproductive system and is helpful in oligospermia, premature ejaculation, Spermatorrhoea and Erectile Dysfunction.

It is basically indicated to bring about the required improvement in blood circulation with a marked control of hypertension. Due to the combination of Rasa-sindur (a powerful anti-oxidant , known to prevent the oxidative wear and tear of critical tissues ), Suvarna Bhasma, Roupya Bhasma, Abhrak Bhasma which are heart tonic, strengthen the myocardium and the vascular network, without elevating blood pressure. It also has Motipishti, which is a brain tonic, heart tonic and has mild tranquilizing effect on the nervous system. It helps patient to cope up with the negative thoughts. It  contains Vanga bhasma which strengthens the muscles and nerves.

## Indications
1. Stroke
1. hypertension
1. diabetes
1. erectile dysfunction
1. oligospermia
1. kidney disorders
1. vata disorders

## Ingredients
Ras sindur, Motipishti, Suvarna bhasma, Abhrak bhasma, Loh bhasma, Suvarnamakshika bhasma, Praval Pishti, Vanga bhasma, Withania somnifera, Syzygium aromaticum, Myristica fragrans
Aloe vera juice, Solanum nigrum juice.
