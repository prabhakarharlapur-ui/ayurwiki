# Bergenia ciliata (Haw ) Sternb - Pashanabhedha

[TOC]

**Pashanabhedha** consists of rhizomes of Bergenia ciliata (Haw.) Sternb., Syn. Bergenia ligulata (Wall.) Engl. (Fam. Saxifragaceae), a small perennial herb found
throughout temperate Himalayas from Bhutan to Kashmir at an altitude between 2000-3000 m and in Khasia hills upto 1200 m altitude.
