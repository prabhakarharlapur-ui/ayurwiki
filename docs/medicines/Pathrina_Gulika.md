# Pathrina Gulika

[TOC]

![*PATHRINA TABLETS*](../images/Small_708931512.JPG)

## Importance
Useful in Inflammatory, Infective and Obstructive Uropathies e.g. Urinary Retention / Anuria, Pyuria, Renal / Ureteric / Cystic Calculii, Cystitis, Crystalluria, Dysuria, UTI, Renal / Ureteric Colic, Polyuria / Frequency etc.

## Dosage
2-4 Tab Three times with Luke warm water or as directed by physician.

## Indications
1. Urinary Retention
1. Renal/Ureteric/ Cystic Calculi
1. Renal/Ureteric Colic
1. Polyuria/ Frequency
1. Cystitis
