# Patragasava

[TOC]

It is an uterine tonic. It is useful in leucorrhoea and menorrhagia.

## Indications
Menorrhagia
leucorrhoea
dysmenorrhoes
prolapse of uteues.

## Dose
4 tab 2 times

## Ingredients
