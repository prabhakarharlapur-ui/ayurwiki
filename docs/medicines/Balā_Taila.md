## Method of preparation
* Take all ingredients of pharmacopoeial quality.
* Treat Tila taila to prepare [mūrcchita](mūrcchita.md) Tila taila.
* A day prior to the [sneha pāka](sneha_pāka.md), iniate to prepare [Mastu](../Mastu.md).
* Wash, clean, dry the ingredients numbered 1 to 3 of the Formulation Composition, powder separately and pass through 355 µm I. S. sieve (sieve number 44) ([kvātha dravya](kvātha_dravya.md)).
* Add water for decoction to the kvātha dravya and soak for 4 h, heat and reduce the volume to one-tenth. Filter through muslin cloth to obtain [kvātha](kvātha.md).
* Wash and clean the cut pieces of [Ikṣu](Ikṣu.md), and squeeze the juice and strain through muslin cloth to obtain Ikṣu [svarasa](svarasa.md).

## Description
* Medicated oil, light golden yellow in color with a pleasant odour.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed containers, protected from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Chardi (Emesis), Gulma (Abdominal lump), Kāsa (Cough), Śvāsa (Asthma), Jvara (Fever), Mūrcchā (Syncope), Kṣata (Wound), Kṣaya (Pthisis), Apasmāra (Epilepsy), Vātavyādhi (Diseases due to Vata dosha), Plīhā (Splenic disease), Śoṣa (Cachexia).

## Dose
* 5-10 ml per day in divided doses; for external use as advised by the physician.
