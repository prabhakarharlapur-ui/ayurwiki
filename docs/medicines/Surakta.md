## Importance
REMOVES PIMPLES AND DARK SPOTS,HELPS TO THIN BLOOD AND MAINTAIN HEALTHY BLOOD CIRCULATION,SKIN SUPPORT SYSTEM FROM INSIDE OUT,LIMINATES LIVER SPOTS AND SPIDER VEINS,NATURAL HEALING FOR VIRAL AND BACTERIAL SKIN ERRUPTIONS, HELPS SKIN TO BE LUSTROUS, SMOOTH, AND SILKY WITH NATURAL GLOW, HELP S CONTROL WRINKLES AS AN ANTI-AGING, PROVIDES OVERALL BODY HEALTH, SUPPORTS PROPER LIVER AND KIDNEY FUNCTIONS.

## Dosage
5-10 ml twice or three times in a day or as directed by physician.

## Indications
1. Remove Pimples and boils
1. Purifies blood
1. Gives you shining and lustrous skin
1. Skin toner
