# Ayurvediv medicines

Ayurvediv medicines are made of [Ayurvedic medicine](../concepts/Ayurvedic_medicine.md) herbs which cures the following health problems.

* **Sishu Kalp** - It plays magic in stomach ache, vomitting of clotted milk, diarrhoea,cough & cold, indigestion, whopping cough, fever and all types of stomach diseases. It improves digestive system and also helps in liver problems.
