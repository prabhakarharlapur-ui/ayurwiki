# Kasamrit

[TOC]

![*KASAMRIT*](../images/Small_248684913.jpg)

## Importance
Effective herbal syrup for cold, cough, asthma, asthmatic cough & bronchitis. Kasamrit herbal can be mixed with Baidyanath Sitopladi churn for a more potent cough medicine.

## Dosage
1 to 2 teaspoon full or as directed by the physician

## Indications
1. Effective in chronic & actual bronchitis besides whooping & asthmatic cough
1. Liquefies and expels viscid secretion in distressing and irritating dry cough
1. Ensure relief in respiratory tract infections
