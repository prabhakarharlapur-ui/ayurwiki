# Abhayāriṣṭa

[TOC]

## Method of preparation
* Take the raw material of pharmacopoeial quality.
* Wash, dry and powder the ingredients numbered 1 and 3 ([Kvātha Dravya](Kvātha_Dravya.md)) of the formulation composition and pass through the sieve number 44 to obtain coarse powder. Wash and clean the ingredient numbered 2 and 4 ([Kvātha](../Kvātha.md) Dravya) of the formulation composition.
* Clean, dry and powder the ingredients numbered 7 to 16 ([Prakṣepa Dravya](Prakṣepa_Dravya.md)) of the formulation composition individually and pass through the sieve number 85 to obtain fine powder.
* Add specified amount of water to the [Kvātha Dravya](Kvātha_Dravya.md), soak overnight, heat, reduce to one fourth and filter through muslin cloth to obtain [Kvātha](../Kvātha.md).

## Description
* Clear dark brown liquid without frothing and significant sedimentation; with aromatic odour and bitter taste.

## Storage
* Store in a cool place in tightly closed amber coloured bottle, protect from light and moisture.

## Therapeutic uses
* Arsta (Piles), Udara (Diseases of abdomen), Mitravibandha (Retention of urine), Agnimandya (Digestive impairment); Varcovibandha (Constipation).

## Dose
* 15 – 30 ml orally with equal amount of water after meals twice a day.
