# Embelia ribes Burm.f - Vidhanga

[TOC]

Vidhanga consists of dried mature fruits of Embelia ribes Burm. f. (Fam.Myrsinaceae), large scandent shrub with long slender, flexible branches, distributed
throughout hilly parts of India upto 1600 m,

## Chemical Composition
Benzoquinones, alkaloid (Christembine), tannin and essential oil
