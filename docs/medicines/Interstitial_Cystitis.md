# Interstitial Cystitis

![*Interstitial Cystitis*](../images/Package-for-Leucoderma-1.jpg)

Interstitial cystitis is a painful condition. Pain occurs in the bladder and the pelvic area. Scientists have also used the term bladder pain syndrome for this condition because symptoms vary from person to person. Every individual suffering from this condition presents a different set of symptoms. It refers to all kinds of pain that may occur during urination. A person may experience pain in the pelvic region that extends downwards. There is urgency of urination and it becomes difficult for a person to control urination. Frequency increases especially at night. Any bacteria or virus is not responsible for causing this problem.In this condition, the bladder becomes stiff. Some people experience that their bladder is not able to hold urine and this causes frequent urination. People with severe condition often have to urinate many times at night. It is more commonly found in women as compared to men. It is most commonly seen in women above 18 years of age.
