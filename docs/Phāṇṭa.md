# Phāṇṭa

[TOC]

The drug is powdered first. Four times the quantity of boiling water is added to it and kept for some time. When cooled, it is filtered and used. The **Phāṇṭa** is the infusion obtained by pouring four times of boiling water on the powdered drug(s) and strained when cooled.
