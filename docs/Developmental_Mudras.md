# Developmental Mudras

[TOC]

**Developmental Mudras** :
Some mudras are practised to develop and maintain radiant health. These [Mudra](Mudra.md) can also be performed by ailing people to regain health. These mudras peace and to remove tension.

## Eaxamples
1. **[Jnana Mudra](Jnana_Mudra.md)**:Performed to increase brain power,improve memory, attaining peace and to remove tension.
1. **[Prithvi mudra](Prithvi_mudra.md)**:Performed to attain balanced health of 5 sense organs,good blood circulation and for energy for happy disposition and alertness, for providing vitamins to the body.
1. **[Apana Mudra](yoga/mudras/Apana_Mudra.md)**:For good digestion, for healthy gums and teeth, for healthy excretory system, for elimination of wastes and for immunity.
1. **[Prana mudra](Prana_mudra.md)**:For removal of fatigue, for provision of vitamins, for immunity, for stamina and vigour, for maintaining health of the eyes, for helping other mudras to be effective.
1. **[Dhyana mudra](yoga/mudras/Dhyana_mudra.md)**:For peace of mind , to avoid bloodpressure and heart attack.
1. **[Shoonya Vayu mudra](Shoonya_Vayu_mudra.md)**:Everyday perform after meals to prevent flatulence, gas problem and also to prevent all kinds of ailments.
