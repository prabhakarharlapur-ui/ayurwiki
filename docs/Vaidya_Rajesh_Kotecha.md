# Vaidya Rajesh Kotecha

Padmashree shree Vaidya Rajesh Kotecha ji is the Secretary to ministry of AYUSH, Government of India. Formerly he was vice chancellor of prestigious Gujarat Ayurveda university, Jamnagar.
