# KukkuṭaPuṭa

[TOC]

The **KukkuṭaPuṭa** is an arrangement to heat the processed material in śarāva sampuṭa by keeping it on the ground and by pilling vanyapala around it to a height of 21.1/2 cm, each in lenght, breadth and depth, with a śarāva sampuṭa in the formulary.
