# Rāja yoga

**Rāja yoga** is the achievement of union with the supreme mind by defeating its enemies. The chief of these enemies are: Kāma (passion or lust), Krodha (anger or wrath), Lobha (greed), Moha (Delusion), Mada (Pride), Matsara (Jealousy or anvy).
