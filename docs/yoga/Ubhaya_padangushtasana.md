## Technique
1. Lie on your back. Keep your feet together and hands by the side of your hips, head resting on the ground.
1. Exhale and come to Halasana / Plow Pose.
1. Extend your arms and take them behind your head.
1. Hold both your big toes and stretch your legs.
1. Inhale and slowly stretch your legs to point your toes towards the ceiling.
1. Lift your upper body and balance your weight on your sitting bones.
1. Push your head back slightly and focus in between your eyebrows.
1. Stay in this pose for 3 long breaths.
