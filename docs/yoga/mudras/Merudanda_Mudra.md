# Merudanda Mudra

[TOC]

Merudanda means - Spinal column. This mudra removes the strain in the back. This mudra is formed differently by each hand.

## Formation
* Right hand - Thumb finger, middle finger and little finger tips touch together. Index finger and ring finger stay extended.
* Left hand  - Place the thumb joint on the nail of the index finger.

## Effects
Space and water elements are balanced hence the vertibrae can move flexibly with the help of wind. Placing the index finger tip at the thumb joint helps in adjusting the level of wind.

## Benefits
1. The following strains on the back are relieved by performing the merudanda mudra:
1. Strain on the back due to gardening.
1. Strain on the back due to house cleaning.
1. Strain on the back due to working for extended period of time on computers.
1. Continuous mental strain, fears, heavy meals, little sleep,little exercise also cause back pain. Such pain is also relieved by performing this mudra.
1. Slip disc problem will be set right by presistant practice of this mudra daily for 30 minutes followed by 10 minutes of prana mudra.
