# Gayatri Mudra

[TOC]

From ancient times **mantras** and **mudras** together have been used to enhance health. Gayatri mantra has from time immemorial maintained its supermacy and unique position among all mantras. Gayatri mantra kindles the power within and is able to ward off the deadly diseases like **CANCER**.
Keshav Deo the scholar and expert in mudra science claims that - along with Gayatri mantra 32 mudras are to be performed to prevent and cure the deadly disease cancer.
Keshav Deo states that repeated performance of all Gayatri Mudras circulates life giving power into the body which prevents the joining of the negative and positive cells of the body.
There are 32 vertebrae in the spine. The 32 mudras influence the health of these 32 vertebrae. There are innumerable nerves and arteries going through these vertebrae to various regions of the body. These nerves and arteries are purified through the mudras and the Gayatri japa. The negative and positive cells which have been destroyed by the disease come back to life and are altered, as a result of which pure power of life starts flowering in the body. So the incurable disease gradually starts getting destroyed.
