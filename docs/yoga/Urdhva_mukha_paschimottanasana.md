## Technique
1. Start the Asana by sitting in the Dandasana (Staff Pose), after that lay down gently on the floor.
1. Breathe in and raise your arms to overhead. Breathe out and raise your legs straight off the floor.
1. Lift up your hips and take your feet to the hands, grab your big foot toe or the sole.
1. Inhale and keep your spine straight (during the process, it’s important that your spine should be erect).
1. Exhale, curl in and roll forward to maintain the balance.
1. Breathe in, as you balance well, set your eyes upwards (gaze upward).
1. Breathe out, fold your midsection (torso) to the legs (completely try to touch your torso to your legs, same as in Paschimottanasana). Use your limbs strength to maintain the balance and remain in the pose with five breath and it’s a final position of this pose.
