## Technique
1. Use both hands to grab your leg.
1. Extend your leg towards the floor as far as it feels comfortable arching your back to maintain a leg hold about midway between your knee and ankle.
1. Breath normally and hold the pose as long as it feels comfortable. Release your leg and straighten your back. Repeat on the other side.
