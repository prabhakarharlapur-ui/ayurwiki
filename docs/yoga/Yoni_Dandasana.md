# Yoni Dandasana

**Yoni Dandasana** is an Asana. It is translated as Womb Staff Pose from Sanskrit.

The name of this pose comes from "yoni" meaning "womb", "danda" meaning "stick or staff", and "asana" meaning "posture" or "seat". It is a pose that is close to Mulabandhasana and has similar benefits and cautions.

## Benefits and Cautions
This advance pose has the following benefits: stretches the knees and inner ankles, opens the inside of the hips and thighs.

Be careful while doing this pose if you have any ankle, knee or hip injuries.
