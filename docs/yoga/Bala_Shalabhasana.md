# Bala Shalabhasana

[TOC]

**Bala Shalabhasana**  is an [Asanas](../Asanas.md). It is translated as Baby Grasshopper Pose from Sanskrit.
The name of this pose comes from "bala" meanind "child", "shalabha" meaning "locust, grasshopper", and "asana" meaning "posture" or "seat".
## Benefits and Cautions
* This pose has the following benefits: strengthens the legs, stretches the outside of the thighs and lower back, creates a sense of balance.
* Be careful while doing this pose if you have any spinal, knee, ankle, or hip injuries.
