# List of reference books on Ayurveda

[TOC]

This page is to list references and sources for information on Ayurveda.

## Books, references on standards and preparations
* **The Ayurvedic Pharmacopedia of India** (Volumes I - VI) - *abbr* A.P.I - Under the Drugs and Cosmetics Act of India (1940), the volumes outline the standards and various formulations which are amended by Ayurvedic Pharmacopedia Committee, with the Chairman authorised to issue amendments to the same.
* **Quality control methods for medicinal plant materials** (1998) by World Health Organisation. (Look for external links section below for the PDF).
