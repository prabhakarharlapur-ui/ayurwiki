## Śodhana Composition
| Gandhaka | Sulphur |  | 1 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Godugdha | Cow milk | Pl. | QS |

## Method
Gandhaka melted in an iron pan smeared with ghee and is poured in a pot containing godugdha and collected when cooled. it is washed, dried and then kept for use.
