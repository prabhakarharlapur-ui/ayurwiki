## Śodhana Composition
| Viṣa (Vatsanābha) | - | Rt. | 1 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Gomūtra | - | - | QS |

## Method
Small pieces of Vatsanābha are bundled in thin muslin cloth and soaked in gomūtra for three days, replacing the latter everyday. Then they are washed and dried.
