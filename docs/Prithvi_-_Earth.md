# Prithvi - Earth

[TOC]

**Prithvi** element is the constituent of body mass, skin, hair, nails, bones, cartilages, muscles etc.

## Quality
* Smell

## Organ
* Nose

## Properties
* Substatial, Heavy, Hard and with only little, downward movement.

## Function
* The Earth element is predominates, giving structure to the cell. This element is the constituent of body mass, skin, hair, nails, bones, cartilages, muscles etc. An excess of earth element causes obesity, while a deficiency causes emaciation.Earth (Prithvi) is associated with the smell and therefore the node. Disorders like leanness, prostation and low immunity can be helped by the manipulation of the earth element.
