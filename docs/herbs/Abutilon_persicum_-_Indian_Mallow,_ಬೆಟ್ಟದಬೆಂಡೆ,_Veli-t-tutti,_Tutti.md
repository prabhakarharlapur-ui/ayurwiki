# Abutilon persicum - Indian Mallow, ಬೆಟ್ಟದಬೆಂಡೆ, Veli-t-tutti, Tutti

[TOC]

![*Bettabende*, *Abutilon persicum*](../images/Abutilon_persicum_(Burm.f.)_Merr._(6781264049).jpg)
**Abutilon persicum** is a large erect shrub, growing up to 1–2 m high. The stem yields a long and silky fibre which can be used to make rope. It can be found growing in roadside clearings and open areas in evergreen and semi-evergreen forests of the [Western ghats](Western_ghats.md) and other parts of India and south-east Asia excluding the cold regions.

## Uses
Kidney gravel, Ulcers, Coughs, Toothaches, Abscesses.

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Alternate, Leaves pubescent, lower leaves ovate-cordate, to 15 cm long; upper ovate to lanceolate, crenate-dentate, velutinous below and glabrescent above, basally  5-9 nerved.

### Flower
bisexual, 3.5 - 4.5 cm across, Yellow, Many, Petals yellow, obovate. FLowering starts post monsoon, November to April

### Fruit
Mericarp, Mericarps 5, Puberulous, Fruiting: November to May
