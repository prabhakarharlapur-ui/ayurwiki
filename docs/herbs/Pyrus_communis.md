# Pyrus communis

[TOC]

![*Aloe Vera*](../images/Pyrus_communis_a1.jpg)
**Pyrus communis** is a deciduous tree with a broad, oval crown or occasionally a shrub. It can range in height from 5 - 30 metres tall. A very variable plant in all its characters.
