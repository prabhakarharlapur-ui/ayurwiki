# Canarium strictum - Mandadhupa

[TOC]

![*Mandadhupa*, *Canarium strictum*](../images/Canarium_strictum_1.JPG)
**Mandadhupa** is a tall evergreen tree with pinnate leaves. It can found growing along rivers and streams in the [western ghats](western_ghats.md). New leaves emerge bright red. Resin from the stem is harvested.
## Uses
Psoriasis, Skin diseases, Arthritis, Fever, Epilepsy, Asthma

## Parts Used

## Identification
### Leaf
Pinnate, Alternate, Leaves imparipinnate, alternate; stipules obscure; leaflets 7-11, opposite.

### Flower
Polygamous, Axillary panicles, Pale yellow, Male flowers; pedicels short; calyx tube campanulate, pubescent without, 5 mm.

### Fruit
A drupe, 3.5 x 1.5 cm, Ellipsoid, dark blue, 1-3-celled; seeds 1-3.
