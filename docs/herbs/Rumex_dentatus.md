# Rumex dentatus

[TOC]

![*Rumex dentatus*](../images/Rumex_dentatus_(4937495852).jpg)
**Rumex dentatus** is a Annual/Biennial up to 0.60 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Cutaneous disorders.

## Parts Used
, stem, leaves, Root.

## Chemical Composition
It contains the compounds were obtained and identified as helonioside A (1), gallic acid (2), isovanillic acid (3), p-hydroxycinnamic acid (4), succinic acid (5), n-butyl-beta-D-fructopyranoside (6), quercetin (7), hexadecanoic acid 2, 3-dihydroxy propyl ester (8), beta-sitosterol (9) and daucosterol.

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in most soils but prefers a deep fertile moderately heavy soil that is humus-rich, moisture-retentive but well-drained and a position in full-sun or part shade.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Moist valleys, Mountain slopes.
