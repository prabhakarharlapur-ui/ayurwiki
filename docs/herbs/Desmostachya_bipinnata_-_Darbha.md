# Desmostachya bipinnata - Darbha

[TOC]

**Desmostachya bipinnata** is a tufted perennial grass growing up to 150 cm tall. The plant is gathered from the wild for its culms, which are used medicinally. The plant is also grown as a soil stabilizer. The culms play a role in Hindu ceremonies.
## Uses
Dysentery, Menorrhagia.

## Parts Used
Stems, Culms.
