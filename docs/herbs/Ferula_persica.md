# Ferula persica

[TOC]

![](../images/Panorama_di_Castiglione_di_Sicilia.JPG)
**Ferula persica** is a perennial plant that can grow up to 1.00 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Rheumatism, Backache.

## Parts Used
Gum.

## Chemical Composition
Fresh plant materials, crude extracts and isolated components of F. persica have shown a wide spectrum of pharmacological properties including anti-pigmentation in Serratia marcescens, cytotoxic, antibacterial, anti-fungal, anti-leishmanial, cancer chemopreventive, reversal of multi-drug resistance, anti-inflammatory and lipoxygenase inhibitory activity.
