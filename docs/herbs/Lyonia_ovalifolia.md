# Lyonia ovalifolia

[TOC]

![](../images/Lyonia_ovalifolia_NP.JPG)
**Lyonia ovalifolia** is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 2.00 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Skin diseases, External parasites.

## Parts Used
Young leaves, stem, leaves, Root.

## Chemical Composition
Phytochemical investigation on the twigs and leaves of Lyonia ovalifolia led to the isolation of thirty-four compounds, including six grayanane diterpenoids (1–6), sixteen ursane triterpenoids etc.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings of half-ripe wood, Air layering.

## How to plant/cultivate
Grow in dappled shade in moist humus-rich lime-free soils and mulch annually with leafmould.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Sunny places on hills, On the edges of oak, On the edges of pine, On the edges of rhododendron woods.
