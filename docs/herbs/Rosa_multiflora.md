# Rosa multiflora

[TOC]

![](../images/Rosa_multiflora_rose.JPG)
**Rosa multiflora** is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 5.00 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Sores, Constipation, Articular pain, Ulcers, Wounds, Sprains, Injuries, , , , .

## Parts Used
Fruits, Seeds, Young leaves, Young shoots.

## Chemical Composition
The fruit of many members of this genus is a very rich source of vitamins and minerals, especially in vitamins A, C and E, flavanoids and other bio-active compounds.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings of half-ripe wood, Cuttings of mature wood, Air layering, Division of suckers.

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in most soils, preferring a circumneutral soil and a sunny position. Grows well in heavy clay soils. Succeeds on poor soils. Dislikes water-logged soils.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
On slopes, On scrub.
