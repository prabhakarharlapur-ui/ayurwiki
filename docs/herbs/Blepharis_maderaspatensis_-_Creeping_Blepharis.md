# Blepharis maderaspatensis - Creeping Blepharis

[TOC]

![](../images/(Blepharis_maderaspatensis)_at_Kambalakonda_08.JPG)
**Blepharis maderaspatensis** is a Prostrate, Creeping, Wiry plant. It will rooting at the nodes. It is seen commonly on Slopes among rocks and Poor gravelly soil on the hills up to 1,400 m.
## Uses
Wounds, Mouth ulcer, Bone's Fracture, Nerve weakness.

## Parts Used
Leaf, Seed.

## Chemical Composition
It contains the following constituents Diphenyl, Picrylhydrazyl and The Enzymes α-glucosidase, Porcine Pancreatic Lipase, Mushroom Tyrosinase were purchased from Sigma Aldrich.
