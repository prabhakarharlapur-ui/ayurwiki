# Anemone obtusiloba

[TOC]

![*Abrus precatorius*, * Gunjaa*](../images/Abrus_precatorius_(1463017430).jpg)
**Anemone obtusiloba** is a perennial herb which is very variable, with 2-5 cm buttercup flowers in colors white, blue or yellow.
## Uses
Contusions, Blistering agent, Vomiting, Rheumatism.

## Parts Used

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in ordinary garden soil but prefers a rich sandy loam[1]. Requires a well-drained humus-rich soil in full sun[200]. Requires a warm sheltered position.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Moist open slopes
