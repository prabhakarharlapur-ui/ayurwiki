# Cassytha filiformis - Akashavalli

[TOC]

![*Akashavalli*, *Cassytha filiformmis*](../images/Cassytha_filiformis_flower.jpg)
**Akashavalli**is leafless parasitic climbing twiner, that can be seen growing in South India in very dry gravelly localities.
## Uses
Dysentery, Skin diseases, Hair problems, Dairrhea

## Parts Used
Stem

## Chemical Composition
The plant contains aporphine alkaloids.
