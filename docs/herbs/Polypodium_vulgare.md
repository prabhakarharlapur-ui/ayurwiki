# Polypodium vulgare

[TOC]

![](../images/Puszcza_Notecka24.JPG)
**Polypodium vulgare** is a Evergreen Fern up to 0.30 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Hepatitis, Jaundice, Indigestion, Skin rashes, Pleurisy, Sore throats, Stomach aches, Liver diseases, Inflammations.

## Parts Used
Roots.

## Mode of Propagation
Spores, Division in spring.

## How to plant/cultivate
Easily grown in most light soils. Prefers a soil of leaf mould and a cool but not too moist clay. Prefers a cool damp shady position. Thrives in dry shade.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Especially in humid shady conditions.
