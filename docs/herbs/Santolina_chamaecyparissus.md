# Santolina chamaecyparissus

[TOC]

![](../images/Santolinachamae.jpg)
**Santolina chamaecyparissus** is an evergreen shrub that can grow up to 0.60 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Indigestion, Menstrual problems, Sting bites, Wounds.

## Parts Used
Leaves, Flowering stems.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings of half-ripe side shoots, Division in spring.

## How to plant/cultivate
An easy and undemanding plant that does not require a rich soil, though it strongly dislikes wet conditions around the roots.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Dry ground, Stony banks, On Rocks.
