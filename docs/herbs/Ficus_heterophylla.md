# Ficus heterophylla

[TOC]

![](../images/Ficus_heterophylla_25.JPG)
**Ficus heterophylla** is a shrub, often prostrate. It grows up to 3 metres tall. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine.
## Uses
Rheumatism, Ear infections.
