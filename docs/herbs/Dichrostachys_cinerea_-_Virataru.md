# Dichrostachys cinerea - Virataru

[TOC]

![*Virataru*, *Dichrostachys cinerea*](../images/Dichrostachys_cinerea_(8444618244).jpg)

**Virataru** is a deciduous or semi-deciduous shrub. It can grow upto 1 - 8 metres tall. The plant is covered with spines and is sometimes suckering and thicket-forming. A true multi-purpose tree, providing food, medicines, fuel and various commodities. This shrub is belongs to Memosaceae family.
## Uses
Diarrhea, Excessive thirst, Kidney stones, Gynecological disorders, Urinary retention.

## Parts Used
Bark, Root, Heartwood

## Chemical Composition
It contain apigenin-7-O-apiosyl (1 → 2) glucoside (1) (Liet al., 1997), chrysoeriol-7-O-apiosyl (1 → 2) glucoside (2) (Lin etal, 2007) and the major compound clovamid.

## Identification
### Leaf
Bipinnate, Alternate, Stipulate; stipules 2, minute, lateral.

### Flower
Polygamous, Solitary or axillary spikes, Pink and yellow, 10, Upper flowers bisexual, yellow; lower ones neutar, pink or purple.

### Fruit
A Pod, Linear, flat, coiled, continuous within, indehiscent or opening from apex, Seeds 4-6, ovoid, compressed.
