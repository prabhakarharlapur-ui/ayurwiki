[TOC]

![](../images/Blooming_stages_of_gardenia_flower_01.jpg)
## Uses
Indigestion, Fever, Mammary glands, Epilepsy, Pimples, Tubercular fistula, Ringworm, Cholera, Smallpox.

## Parts Used
Roots, Fruits.

## Chemical Composition
Roots: D-mannitol, its hexaacetate, its hexabenzoate, gardenin A (mp. 163 degree), gardenin B (mp. 177 degree), gardenin E (mp. 230 degree), oleanolic acid, alpha-amyrin and beta-sitosterol. Bark: saponin. Bark & wood: beta-sitosterol, hederagenin, Me-ester of oleanolic and gypsogenic acids.
