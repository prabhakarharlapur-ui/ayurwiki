# Geranium nepalense

[TOC]

![](../images/Chunitro_Ghans_(Nepali-_चुनित्रो_घाँस)_(7863946834).jpg)
**Geranium nepalense** is a perennial plant that can grow up to 1 metres tall.
It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Nervous diseases, Numbness of the limbs, Pains, Rheumatism, Renal diseases, Renal diseases.
