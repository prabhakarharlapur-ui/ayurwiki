# Argemone mexicana - Svarnakshiri

[TOC]

![*Svarnakshiri*, *Adenanthera pavonina*](../images/Argemone_mexicana_2749.jpg)
**Svarnakshiri** is an erect, branched, annual to perennial plant. It grows up to  60cm tall. The plant has a wide range of traditional medicinal applications and also provides an oil. This plant is belongs to Papaveraceae family.
## Uses
Wound healing, Malaria, Fever, Worm infestation, Blood disorders

## Parts Used
Root, Seeds, Latex

## Chemical Composition
The herb contains isoquinoline alkaloids. The fresh latex contains proteindissolving constituents. Latex contains alkaloid berberine (0.74%), protopine (0.36%) and free amino acids. Sanguinarine is the toxic
factor in seeds.

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Alternate, Leaves alternate, 12-30 x 4-10 cm, dissected, semi-amplexicaule, membranous, margins spiny, midrib thick. Sap yellow

### Flower
Bisexual, Terminal, solitary, Pale yellow, Many, 4.5 cm across, yellow; sepals 2-3; petals 4-6; stamens many; ovary 1 celled, ovules many, stigma 5- lobed.

### Fruit
A capsule, 4 x 2cm, Oblong, spiny, dehiscing apically downwards; seeds rugose.
