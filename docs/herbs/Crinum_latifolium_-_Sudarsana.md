# Crinum latifolium - Sudarsana

[TOC]

![*Sudarsana*, *Crinum latifolium*](../images/Crinum_latifolium_(1).jpg)
**Sudarsana**is a bulbous plant with lance shaped leaves and beautiful bell shaped white flowers. The plant stays dormant for several months of the year, leaves and flowers appear before the monsoon.
## Uses
Fungal infections, Antidote for poisoning, Fever, Joint pain, Ear ache

## Parts Used
Leaves, Rhizome

## Chemical Composition
The alkaloids, crinafoline and crinafolidine, have been isolated from the plant. These along with crinafoline methochloride exhibited significant tumour-inhibiting activity in vivo.

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Rootstock bulbous, ca. 6 cm across. Leaves many, densely clustered, 30-45 x 7-8 cm, flat or widely channelled, oblong, curved, acute or obtuse; sheath closed, membranous.

### Flower
Unisexual, Stout peduncled umbel, White and pink, 10, Flowers sessile, 12-16 together, on stout peduncled umbel.

### Fruit
A capsule, Oblong, Seeds many
