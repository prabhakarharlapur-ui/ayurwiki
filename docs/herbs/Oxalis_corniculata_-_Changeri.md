# Oxalis corniculata - Changeri

[TOC]

![*Changeri*, *Oxalis corniculata*](../images/Oxalis_corniculata_1.JPG)
**Changeri**, is a herbaceous creeping plant. The leaves are bright green and trifoliate, flowers are bright yellow. The whole plant is edible and has a very sour taste.
## Uses
Scurvy, Antidote for poison, Fever, Inflammation, Insomnia

### Food
Oxalis corniculata can be used in Food.  Leaves are eaten raw as a salad or cooked as vegetable.

## Parts Used
Flowers, Leaves

## Identification
### Leaf
Trifoliate, Alternate, Leaflets 0.5-1.5 x 0.6-2 cm, broadly obcordate, apex emarginate, base cuneate; petioles 1.5-3 cm long.

### Flower
Bisexual, Solitary or in axillary umbels, Yellow, 10, Peduncles 4-10 cm long; pedicels 0.5-1 cm long; bracts 2, 3-4 mm long, linear-lanceolate.

### Fruit
A capsule, 0.8-1.5 x 0.3-0.5 cm, Linear, puberulous outside, Seeds many, minute, brown.

## Mode of Propagation

## How to plant/cultivate
A weed of gardens and other moist shady areas. Grows readily from seed and roots along stem. Oxalis corniculata is available throughout the year.
