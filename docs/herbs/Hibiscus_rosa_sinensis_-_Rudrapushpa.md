[TOC]

![**Hibiscus**](../images/Hibiscus_rosa-sinensis_white-pink.jpg)

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Dorsiventral, Both the glandular and stellate types of trichomes are present. Stomata are of ranunculaceous or rubiaceous typa, present on the lower surface

### Flower
Unisexual, 2-4cm long, Yellow, 5-20, Flower powder shows spheroidal, pantoporate, pore-circular pollen grains; stellate trichomes single,elongated, conical or twisted and convoluted

### Fruit
Simple, 4-8 cm, Clearly grooved lengthwise, Lowest hooked hairs aligned towards crown, With hooked hairs, many

## How to plant/cultivate
A common way to propagate tropical hibiscus is through stem cuttings. The cuttings should be about 8 inches long, with the stem cut at an angle. The cut should be made directly through a leaf node. All but the top two leaves must be removed, as well as any buds or flowers. The planting medium commonly used is coarse builder's sand or perlite.
