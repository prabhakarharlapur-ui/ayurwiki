# Chrozophora plicata

[TOC]

![](../images/Chrozophora_plicata_(Vahl)_A.Juss._ex_Spreng._(29096098190).jpg)
**Chrozophora plicata** is a monoecious, annual to short-lived perennial herb. It grows up to 50 cm tall. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine.
## Uses
Jaundice, Wounds.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Seeds.

## Chemical Composition
The  Alkali  and  Alkaline  earth  metal  ions,  mentioned  previously  were present in the powder of Chrozophora plicata leaves in small quantity. The amount of Lithium and Barium ions were present in Chrozophora plicata leaves  more  than  sodium  and  calcium  ions  while potassium ions were present in small quantity.
