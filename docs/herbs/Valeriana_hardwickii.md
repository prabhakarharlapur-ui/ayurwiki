# Valeriana hardwickii

[TOC]

**Valeriana hardwickii** is a perennial plant that can grow up to 1.50 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Epilepsy, Hysteria, Rheumatism, Low blood pressure, Low blood pressure, Blood pressure, Cramps, Hypertension, Irritable bowel syndrome, Liver problems, Eczema.

## Parts Used
Roots.

## Chemical Composition
It contains methylapigenin, 5-hydroxy'-dimethoxyflavone, genkwanin, acacetin, apigenin, quercetin, tricin, farrerol, sosakuranetin, trihydroxy-7-methoxyflavanone, bornyl ferulate, and bornyl caffeate.
