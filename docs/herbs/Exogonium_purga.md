# Exogonium purga

[TOC]

![](../images/Ipomoea_purga_(as_Exogonium_purga)_Bot._Reg._33.49.jpg)
**Exogonium purga** is a stout, perennial herb. Producing annual stems from a large, fusiform, tuberous rootstock. These stems scramble over the ground or twine into nearby vegetation for support.
