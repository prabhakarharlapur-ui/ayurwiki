# Cissus sicyoides - Millionaire vine

[TOC]

![*Millionaire vine*, *Cissus sicyoides*](../images/Starr_060810-8542_Cissus_verticillata.jpg)
**Millionaire vine** is evergreen perennial vine in the Vitaceae family.
## Uses
Cuts, Fever, Snakebites, Gangrene, Thrush, Ulcers, Swellings, Diarrhea, Sore throats

## Parts Used
Leaves, Seeds.

## Chemical Composition
Contains volatile oils, flavonoids, apigenin, luteolin, quercetin, kaempferol, tiliroside, triterpene glycosides including euscapic acid and tormentic acid, phenolic acids, and 3%–21% tannins

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Waxy dark green leaves

### Flower
Unisexual, 8-10 mm in diameter, Greenish-yellow, 5, Whitish or purple berries subglobose or obovoid

### Fruit
Simple, 7–10 mm, Black grape-like fruit are very showy in fall, Single
