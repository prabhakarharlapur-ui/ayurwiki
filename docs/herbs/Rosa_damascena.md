# Rosa damascena

[TOC]

![](../images/MHNT_-_Rosier_de_Damas.jpg)
**Rosa x damascena** is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 1.50 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Cold, Cancer.

## Parts Used
Young leaves, Petals, Fruits, Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
It contains  β-citronellol (48.2 %), geraniol (17.0 %), β-phenylethyl benzoate (5.4 %) and phenyl ethyl alcohol (5.1 %) as the main components. The MIC values of oil were in the ranges of 0.125–1 μl/ml.

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in most soils, preferring a circumneutral soil and a sunny position. Grows well in heavy clay soils. Dislikes water-logged soils. The plant resists frost.
