# Jacaranda acutifolia

[TOC]

![](../images/Jacaranda_acutifolia0.jpg)
Jacaranda is a deciduous tree with spreading branches making a light crown; it can grow 10 - 20 metres tall. The bole is almost always short and malformed, it can be up to 40 - 50cm in diameter.

## How to plant/cultivate
A plant of the moist tropics and subtropics, where it is found at elevations from 500 - 2,400 metres. It grows best in areas where annual daytime temperatures are within the range 20 - 34°c, but can tolerate 10 - 38°c.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Bushland, Grassland, Wooded ravines, Riverbanks.
