# Thlaspi arvense

[TOC]

![*Thlaspi arvense*](../images/Thlaspi_arvense_001.JPG)
**Thlaspi arvense** is an annual plant that can grow up to 0.60 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Eveys, Renal inflammation, Appendicitis, Vaginal discharges, Carbuncles, Endometriosis, Nausea, Dizziness.

## Parts Used
Young leave, Seeds.
