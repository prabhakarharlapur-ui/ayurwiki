## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Root cuttings.

## How to plant/cultivate
Easily grown in any moist soil in sun or part shade. Prefers a damp position. Grows well in heavy clay soils. Dislikes very light or dry soils. Grows well in the shade of a north-facing wall.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Open slopes, Often along drains.
