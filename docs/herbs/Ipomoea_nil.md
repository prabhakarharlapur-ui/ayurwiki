# Ipomoea nil

[TOC]

![](../images/Ipomoea_nil_(1305555624).jpg)
**Ipomoea nil** is a climbing, herbaceous annual or perennial plant producing stems that either twine into other plants for support or sprawl along the ground. The plant is gathered from the wild for local medicinal use.

## Uses
Oedema, Oliguria, Ascariasis, Constipation, Mental disorders.

### Food
Ipomoea nil can be used in Food. Tender leaves are cooked as vegetable.

## Parts Used
, stem, leaves, Root.

## How to plant/cultivate
The plant is not frost hardy, but can be grown outdoors as a tender annual in temperate zones. Ipomoea nil is available through August to November.
