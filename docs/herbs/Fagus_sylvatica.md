# Fagus sylvatica

[TOC]

![](../images/Buchenwald_1.jpg)
**Fagus sylvatica** is a deciduous tree with a large, dense crown. It can grow upto 30 - 50 metres tall. The tree is harvested from the wild as a food and source of materials.
## Uses
Skin diseases, Toothache.

## Parts Used
Young leaves, Seeds.

## How to plant/cultivate
Fagus sylvatica is a very cold-hardy tree, tolerating temperatures down to around -25°c when dormant.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Grows in woodlands, Soft limestone soils, Chalky limestone soils, Well-drained loams.
