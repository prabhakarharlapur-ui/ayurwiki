# Fraxinus ornus

[TOC]

![](../images/Costigiola-orniello-1.jpg)
**Fraxinus ornus** is a deciduous tree with a very dense, rounded canopy. It can grow up to 9 metres tall. The bole is usually narrow and crooked. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and a medicine.
