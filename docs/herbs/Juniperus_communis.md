# Juniperus communis

[TOC]

![](../images/20161013_Jeneverbessenbos1.jpg)
**Juniperus communis** is a very variable, evergreen shrub or a tree with an erect stem. It can grow up to 12 metres tall, though it is more commonly within the range 1 - 3 metres. The branches can be erect or spreading, so crown shape is very variable.
## Uses
Kidney problems, Bladder problems, Cystitis, Digestive problems, Chronic arthritis, Gout, Rheumatic conditions.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Stems, Leaves.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings of mature wood, Air layering.

## How to plant/cultivate
Juniperus communis is a very cold-hardy plant, able to tolerate temperatures down to around -40°c when fully dormant. However, although the fully dormant plant is very cold-tolerant, the young growth in spring can be damaged by late frosts.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Pine woods, Moorland, Calcareous river banks, Mixed woods.
