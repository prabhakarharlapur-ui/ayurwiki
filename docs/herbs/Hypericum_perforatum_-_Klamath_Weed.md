# Hypericum perforatum - Klamath Weed

[TOC]

![*Klamath Weed*, *Springtime St. John's Wort*](../images/Springtime_St._John's_Wort_(12610079565).jpg)
**Hypericum perforatum** may be used to refer to any species of the genus Hypericum. it is used for the treatment for depression in humans.
## Uses
Depression, Sciatica, Black cohosh, Menopause, Dermatitis, Anxiety, Pimples, Diarrhea, Sore throats.

## Parts Used
Dried folaige, Whole herb.

## Chemical Composition
The major constituents of the essential oils were α-pinene (12.52–49.96%), β-pinene (6.34–9.70%), (E)-β-ocimene (4.44–12.54%), β-caryophyllene (1.19–5.67%), and germacrene-D (2.34–6.92%).
