# Grangea maderaspatana

[TOC]

![](../images/Grangea_maderaspatana_(L.)_Poir._(5663682158).jpg)
**Grangea maderaspatana** is an annual or occasionally perennial prostrate herb sending out shoots up to 30cm long from a woody taproot. The leaves are harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine.
## Uses
Earache, Indigestion, Womens problems, Wound healing

## Parts Used
Leaves

## Chemical Composition
α-humulene (46.3%), β-caryophyllene (9.3%), α-copaene (8.2%), β-myrcene (4.3%), Z(E)-α-farnesene (3.7%), and calarene (3.5%)
