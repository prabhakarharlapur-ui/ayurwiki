# Uvaria narum

[TOC]

![](../images/Uvaria_narum_-_South-Indian_Uvaria_flower_at_Mayyil_(3).jpg)
**Uvaria narum** is a large, woody, climbing shrub. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and source of an essential oil.
## Uses
Various diseases.

## Parts Used
Roots.

## Chemical Composition
It contains On evapouration of the dry ether extract after removal of sodium sulphate, over a water bath yielded 0.9 g (0.11%) of the essential oil.
