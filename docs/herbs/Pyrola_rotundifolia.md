# Pyrola rotundifolia

[TOC]

![*Aloe Vera*](../images/Pyrola_rotundifolia_-_group_01.jpg)
**Pyrola rotundifolia** is a Evergreen Perennial up to 0.25 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Skin diseases, Epilepsy, Urinary antiseptic, Hydrolyzes.

## Parts Used
, stem, leaves, Root.

## Chemical Composition
It is known that phenolic compounds such as tannins and flavonoids are soluble in ethanol; therefore, they can be the major group of active components for the destruction of leukemic cells.

## How to plant/cultivate
Prefers a moist sandy woodland soil. In a cool position with partial shade. Requires a peaty or leafy but not very acid soil that remains moist in the summer.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Especially beech woods, Often on limestone.
