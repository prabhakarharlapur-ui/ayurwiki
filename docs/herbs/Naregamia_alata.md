# Naregamia alata

[TOC]

![](../images/Naregamia_alata_03.JPG)
**Naregamia alata** is a shrub growing 15 - 45cm tall. The plant is gathered from the wild for local medicinal use.
## Uses
Rheumatism, Dysentery, Biliousness.

## Parts Used
stem, leaves, Root.

## Chemical Composition
It contains thirty four compounds were identified by mass spectral database search and comparison of linear retention indices. γ-Himachalene (11.2%), β-Caryophyllene (10.8%) and β-Sesquiphellandrene (10.7%) were the major constituents.
