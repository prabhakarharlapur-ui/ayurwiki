# Potentilla nepalensis

[TOC]

![](../images/Tuinen_Mien_Ruys_(actm)_22.jpg)
**Potentilla nepalensis** is a perennial plant that can grow up to 0.60 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine.
## Uses
Burns, Inflammations, Wounds, Cancer, Infections due to bacteria, Fungi, Diarrhoea.

## Parts Used
Roots.

## Chemical Composition
Phytochemical constituents in the aerial and underground parts. In vitro and in vivo pharmacological studies are reviewed and discussed, focussing on antidiarrhoic.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Division in spring.

## How to plant/cultivate
A very tolerant and easily grown plant, surviving considerable neglect. It grows best in a well-drained loam, preferring a position in full sun but tolerating shade. Prefers an alkaline soil but tolerates a slightly acid soil.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Grazing grounds, Cultivated areas.
