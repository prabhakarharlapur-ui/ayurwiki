# Flacourtia jangomas

[TOC]

![](../images/Lubika_-_ലൂബിക്ക_04.JPG)
**Flacourtia jangomas** is a small, spiny, evergreen tree that branches from very low down on the bole. It can grow up to 10 metres tall,  but it is usually smaller. The plant is cultivated, especially in tropical Asia but also in Tropical America for its edible fruit.
## Uses
Diarrhoea, Dysentery.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Young shoots.
