# Urtica parviflora

[TOC]

![](../images/Urtica_parviflora_06.JPG)
**Urtica parviflora** is a perennial plant that can grow up to 0.50 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Fevers.

## Parts Used
Young leaves, Young shoots.

## How to plant/cultivate
We have very little information on this species and do not know if it will be hardy in Britain, though judging by its native range it could succeed outdoors at least in the milder parts of the country.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Amongst taller herbaceous vegetation, Forests, Moist places of evergreen forests, Along streams, Roadsides.
