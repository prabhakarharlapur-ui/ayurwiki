# Rosa sericea

[TOC]

![](../images/Rosa_sericea_-_Silky_Rose_at_Chelela_Pass_during_LGFC_-_Bhutan_2019_(1).jpg)
**Rosa sericea** is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 2.00 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine.
## Uses
Headaches, Liver complaints, Uterine diseases, Cancer.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Seeds.

## Chemical Composition
The fruit of many members of this genus is a very rich source of vitamins and minerals, especially in vitamins A, C and E, flavanoids and other bio-active compounds.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings of half-ripe wood, Cuttings of mature wood.

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in most soils, preferring a circumneutral soil and a sunny position. Grows well in heavy clay soils. Dislikes water-logged soils.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Forests, Shrubberies, Alpine slopes, On mountain tops and slopes of valleys, On dry sunny places.
