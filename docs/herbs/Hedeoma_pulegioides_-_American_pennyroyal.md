# Hedeoma pulegioides - American pennyroyal

[TOC]

![Pudding grass](../images/Gardenology.org-IMG_2750_rbgs11jan.jpg)
**Hedeoma pulegioides** is an annual plant that can grow up to 0.30 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Digestive disorders, Colds, Whooping cough, Painful menstruation.
