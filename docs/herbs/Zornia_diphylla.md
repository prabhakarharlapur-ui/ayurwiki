# Zornia diphylla

[TOC]

![](../images/Zornia_diphylla.jpg)
**Zornia diphylla** is a densely-branched, prostrate to erect plant with wiry stems up to 50cm long. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine.
## Uses
Dysentery, Venereal diseases.

## Parts Used
Roots.

## Chemical Composition
Isoflavone, hydroxy, methoxyisoflavone, Dihydroxy-4'-methoxyisoflavone, Dihydroxy-4'-methoxyisoflavone, Dihydroxy-8-methoxyisoflavone and Dihydroxy-isoflavone.
