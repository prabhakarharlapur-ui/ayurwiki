# Lilium wallichianum

[TOC]

![](../images/Liliumwallichianumbulbs.jpg)
**Lilium wallichianum** is a Bulb up to 1.80 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine.
## Uses
Pectoral complaints.

## Parts Used

## How to plant/cultivate
Prefers an open free-draining humus-rich loamy soil with its roots in the shade and its head in the sun.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Open slopes, Grassland, Moist shady places.
