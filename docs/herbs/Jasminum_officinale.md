# Jasminum officinale

[TOC]

![](../images/Jasmin.4014.JPG)
**Jasminum officinale** is a Deciduous Climber up to 10.00 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Ringworm, Relieve tension, Sensitive skin, Depression, Nervous tension, Impotence, Frigidity, Menstrual disorders, Weak digestion.

## Parts Used

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings of half-ripe wood, Cuttings of mature wood, Air layering.

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in a good well-drained loam, preferring a sunny position. Very shade tolerant, it succeeds on a north facing wall.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Shrubberies, Forests, Humus-rich soils, Valleys, Ravines.
