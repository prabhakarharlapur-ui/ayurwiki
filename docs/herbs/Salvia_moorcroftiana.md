# Salvia moorcroftiana

[TOC]

**Salvia moorcroftiana** is a perennial plant that can grow up to 0.90 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine
## Uses
Colds, Coughs, Dysentery, Dysentery, Colic, Boils.

## How to plant/cultivate
Requires a very well-drained light sandy soil in a sunny position. Prefers a rich soil. Plants can be killed by excessive winter wet. Closely related to S. argentea.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
