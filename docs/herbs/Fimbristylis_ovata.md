# Fimbristylis ovata

[TOC]

![](../images/Abildgaardia_ovata_crown1_-_Flickr_-_Macleay_Grass_Man.jpg)
**Fimbristylis ovata** is an erect, perennial grass-like plant with short rhizome. The stems are densely tufted, usually 15 - 40cm tall. The plant is sometimes harvested from the wild for local medicinal use.
