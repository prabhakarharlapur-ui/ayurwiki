# Thottea siliquosa - Chakrani

[TOC]

![**Chakrani**](../images/Thottea_siliquosa_flower_-_Chakrani_at_Peravoor_(1).jpg)
**Chakrani** is a slender shrub found in the forests of the [western ghats](western_ghats.md), especially along streams. The roots
of Thottea siliquosa are used in the Indian systems of treatment, as curative for diarrhoea and dysentery, often administered along with lemon juice.  Have also recorded that a paste prepared from the plant with oil is effective against chronic sores and ulcers.

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, 22 x 6cm, Elliptic lanceolate, acuminate, base tapering, subsessile, exstipulate

### Flower
Unisexual, 1.5cm across, dark purple, 12, Flowers in axillary fascicles, 1-5 together

### Fruit
capsule, 12cm x 5mm, The fruit is a long capsule, seeds 2 mm long, trigonous, rugose.
