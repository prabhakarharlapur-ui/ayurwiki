# Rubus sieboldii - Molucca Raspberry

[TOC]

**Rubus sieboldi** is a flowering plant in the genus Rubus family and it is native to Australia, and mid-western Asia, in the Himalaya Mountains and foothills.

## Uses
Menstrual cramps, Fever, colds, Flu, Burns, varicose veins, Conjunctivitis, Diarrhea, Wounds

## Parts Used
Fruits, Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
Cyanidin-3-sophoroside was the most abundant anthocyanin. All quality parameters were significantly affected by genotype. The genotypes could be categorised into three groups. 'Veten' and 'RU984 06038' were characterised by high concentrations of flavonoids

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Round, serrated leaves can get up to 22 cm long

### Flower
Unisexual, 2-4cm long, White, 5, 5 oval shaped white petals

### Fruit
7–10 mm, Red sweet fruit that is liked by birds and rodents, With hooked hairs, Single

## How to plant/cultivate
