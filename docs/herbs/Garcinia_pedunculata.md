# Garcinia pedunculata

[TOC]

![](../images/Garcinia_pedunculata.jpeg)
**Garcinia pedunculata** is a tree growing around 20 metres tall. The bark is thick and corky. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and food. It is sometimes cultivated for its fruit in Bangladesh.
## Uses
Gastric issues, Rheumatic pain.

## Parts Used
Seeds, Leaves.
