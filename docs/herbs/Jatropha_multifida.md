# Jatropha multifida

[TOC]

![](../images/Arbre_corail_feuille.JPG)
**Jatropha multifida** is an evergreen or semi-deciduous, slightly succulent shrub or a tree with a loose, spreading crown; it can grow up to 6 metres tall, though is more likely to be 2 - 3 metres tall in cultivation.
