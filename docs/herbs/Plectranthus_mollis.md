# Plectranthus mollis

[TOC]

![](../images/Soft-stem_Mintleaf_(6256643116).jpg)
**Plectranthus mollis** is an erect, succulent annual plant growing 30 - 50cm tall. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and insect repellant.
## Uses
Haemorrhages, Rheumatism, Mental retardation, Skin conditions, Snakebites, Bleeding, Fever, Cancer, Trauma.

## Parts Used
Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
The main compound was identified as fenchone, followed by α-humulene, piperitenone oxide, cis-piperitone oxide and E-β-farnesene etc.
