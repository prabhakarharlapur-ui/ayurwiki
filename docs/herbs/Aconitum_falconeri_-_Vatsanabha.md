## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Alternate, Scattered, 10 or more, if many, the upper sometimes rather crowded, the intermediate usually very distant, the lowest decayed at the time of flowering; petioles slender, lowest up to 12cm.

### Flower
Bisexual, Raceme, Blue/violet, An erect stiff, usually dense raceme, about 15-20 cm. long, rarely lax and with slender few-flowered ascending additional branches from below

### Fruit
Follicles, Erect and contiguous or slightly diverging upwards, oblong rounded at the top, 14-18 mm. long, 4-5 mm. broad, glabrous, faintly reticulate, Brown, obconic, 3-4 mm
