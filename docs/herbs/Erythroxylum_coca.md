# Erythroxylum coca

[TOC]

![](../images/Coca_(Erythroxylum_coca)_en_Meta_(Colombia)_1.jpg)
**Erythroxylum coca** is a shrub or small tree, with very prominent, sometimes warty lenticels on the branches. It most commonly grows from 1 - 2 metres tall. Widely used as the source of the street drug cocaine, the leaves have long been used by local people as a stimulant to overcome hunger and exhaustion.

Physical stress, Soroche.

## How to plant/cultivate
A plant of moderate to higher elevations in the tropics. It grows best in areas where annual daytime temperatures are within the range 17 - 23°c, but can tolerate 14 - 27°c.
