# Rosa webbiana

[TOC]

![](../images/Rosa_webbiana_JBP.jpg)
**Rosa webbiana** is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 2.00 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine
## Uses
Jaundice, Stomach aches, Cancer.

## Parts Used
Fruits.

## Chemical Composition
The fruit of many members of this genus is a very rich source of vitamins and minerals, especially in vitamins A, C and E, flavanoids and other bio-active compounds.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings of half-ripe wood, Cuttings of mature wood, Division of suckers, Layering.

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in most soils, preferring a circumneutral soil and a sunny position. Grows well in heavy clay soils. Dislikes water-logged soils.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Rocky slopes, On slopes, Grassy places, Among forest.
