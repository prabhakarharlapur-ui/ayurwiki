# Salsola kali

[TOC]

![](../images/Salsola_kali_01.jpg)
**Salsola kali** is an annual plant that can grow up to 0.60 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Blood pressure, Dropsy, Excrescences.

## Parts Used
Young leaves, Stems, Seeds.

## Chemical Composition
It contains ferulic acid (1), p-coumaric acid (2), salicylic acid (3), tricin (4), selagin (5), acanthoside D (6), tricin -7-O-beta-D-glucopyranoside (7), tricin-4'-O-beta-D-apioside (8), isorhamnetin-7-O-beta-D-glucopyranoside (9), isorhamnetin-3-O-beta-D-glucopyranoside (10), isorhamnetin-3-O-alpha-L-arabinopyranosyl (1-->6) -beta-D-glucopyranoside

Seeds

## How to plant/cultivate
Requires a very sunny position in a light or medium well-drained soil. Tolerates maritime exposure. The plant is reported to tolerate an annual precipitation in the range of 26 to 97cm, an annual temperature in the range of 9.2 to 23.8°C and a pH of 7.0 to 7.9.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Non-saline sandy beaches, Avoiding acid soils, Found on dry soils.
