# Rosa centifolia

[TOC]

![](../images/Rosa_centifolia_003.JPG)
**Rosa centifolia** is a deciduous shrub that can grow up to 1.50 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Constipation, Cancer.

## Parts Used
Seeds, Seeds, Petals.

## Chemical Composition
The fruit of many members of this genus is a very rich source of vitamins and minerals, especially in vitamins A, C and E, flavanoids and other bio-active compounds.

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in most soils, preferring a circumneutral soil and a sunny position. Grows well in heavy clay soils. Dislikes water-logged soils.
