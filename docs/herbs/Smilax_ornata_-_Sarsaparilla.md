# Smilax ornata - Sarsaparilla

[TOC]

**Smilax ornata** is a perennial, trailing vine with prickly stems that is native to Mexico and Central America.
## Uses
Tumor, Cough, Cold, Arthritis, Joint pain, Rheumatism, Skin problems, Psoriasis, Toe fungus

## Parts Used
Leaves, Stem.

## Chemical Composition

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings.

## How to plant/cultivate
Prefers well-drained soil in sun or partial shade. Needs minimum 54F.  Propagate by seed, suckers, or division in spring, or by semiripe cuttings in summer.  Harvest roots and rhizomes are lifted by severing larger roots near the crown, leaving smaller roots to increase.  They are dried for use in decoctions, elixirs, liquid extracts, and powders.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Temperate area, Tropical area.
