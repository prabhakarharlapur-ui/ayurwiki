# Rhamnus virgatus

[TOC]

**Rhamnus virgata** is a much-branched, spiny, deciduous shrub or small tree with a dense, rounded crown; it can grow up to 6 metres tall. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of fuel.
## Uses
Affections of the spleen.

## Parts Used
Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
It contains quercetin methyl ether isorhamnetin, as well as rhamnosin, Methyl quercetin, 3-O-methyl quercetin, and rhamnocitrin, were isolated from the aerial parts of Rhamnus lycoides etc.
