# Citrus limon - Bijapuraka, Jambira

[TOC]

![**Lemon**,**Jambira**](../images/Citrus_x_limon_'Variegated_Pink'_-_Lemon.jpg)
**Citrus limon** is a species of small evergreen tree in the flowering plant family Rutaceae and it is native to Asia.
## Uses
Common cold, Cough, Cancer, Kidney stone, Systolic blood pressure, Acid reflux,
Gastritis, Arthritis, Gouty diathesis.

## Parts Used
Leaves, Fruits.

## Chemical Composition
All parts of the plants of citrus sp. contain coumarins and psoralins. The fruits contain flavonoids and limonoids.

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Ovate, Margin of leaves are conspicuously crenulate

### Flower
Bisexual, 2-4cm long, Pale yellow, 5, Flowers solitary or several in fascicles

### Fruit
Ellipsoid to ovoid, 7–10 mm, Fruits are yellow, ellipsoid to ovoid, narrowed at both ends, surface usually coarse and lemon scented, Apex acute, Seeds ovoid
