# Plantago lanceolata

[TOC]

![](../images/Plantago_lanceolata5_ies.jpg)
**Plantago lanceolata** is a perennial plant that can grow up to 0.45 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Bleeding, Diarrhoea, Gastritis, Peptic ulcers, Irritable bowel syndrome, Haemorrhage, Haemorrhoids, Cystitis, Bronchitis, Catarrh, Sinusitis.

## Parts Used
Young leaves, Seeds.
