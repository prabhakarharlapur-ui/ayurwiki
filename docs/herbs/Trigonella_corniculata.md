# Trigonella corniculata

[TOC]

![*Trigonella corniculata*](../images/Trigonella_corniculata—Flora_Batava._Volume_3_(1814).jpg)
Trigonella corniculata is a small, erect to creeping, annual plant. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and a medicine. It is a traditional garden crop in India, grown as a pot-herb and a spice.
## Uses
Swellings, Bruises, Diabetes, Hypercholesterolemia.

## Parts Used
Young leaves, Stem tips.

## Chemical Composition
In addition, its main chemical compounds are saponins, coumarin, fenugreekine, nicotinic acid, phytic acid, scopoletin and trigonelline31.
