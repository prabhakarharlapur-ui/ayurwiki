# Carthamus tinctorius

[TOC]

![](../images/Carthamus_tinctorius_001.JPG)

**Carthamus tinctorius** is an erect, thistle-like, often spined annual plant. It can grow up to 180cm tall. The plant has a deep taproot and a strong stem with many branches, each terminating in a flower.
## Uses
Lower cholesterol, Tumours, Stomatitis, Coronary diseases.

## Parts Used
Young leaves, Seeds.

## How to plant/cultivate
Safflower probably grows best in the semi-arid subtropics, but cultivars have been developed that widen its range and it is now cultivated from the temperate zone to higher elevations in the tropics.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
