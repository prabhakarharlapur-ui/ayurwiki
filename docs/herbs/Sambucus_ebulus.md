# Sambucus ebulus

[TOC]

![](../images/Sambucus_ebulus_(14945578137).jpg)
**Sambucus ebulus** is a perennial plant that can grow up to 1.20 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Kidney complaints, Liver complaints, Swellings, Contusions, Dropsy, Nausea, Vertigo.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Leaves.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Division of suckers.

## How to plant/cultivate
Tolerates most soils, including chalk, but prefers a moist loamy soil. Grows well in heavy clay soils. Tolerates some shade but is best in a sunny position. Tolerates atmospheric pollution and coastal situations.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Waste ground, Hedgerows.
