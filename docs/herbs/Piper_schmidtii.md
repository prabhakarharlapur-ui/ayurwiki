# Piper schmidtii

[TOC]

![**Piper schmidtii**](../images/Piper_schmidtii_at_Mannavan_Shola,_Anamudi_Shola_National_Park,_Kerala_(4).jpg)
**Piper schmidtii** has a climbing shrubs, stem to 1.5 cm thick.
## Uses
Fever, Caugh.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
It has friedelin, beta-sitosterol and its beta-O-glucoside.
