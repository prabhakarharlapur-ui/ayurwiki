# Leucas martinicensis

[TOC]

**Leucas martinicensis** is an erect, usually unbranched, annual, aromatic herb growing from 50 - 100 cm tall. The plant is sometimes gathered from the wild and used as a tea and insect repellent.
## Uses
Gastro-intestinal troubles, Wounds, Sores, Eye ache, Nose ache, Chronic skin diseases, Psoriasis, Scabies, Fevers, Colds, Rheumatism.
