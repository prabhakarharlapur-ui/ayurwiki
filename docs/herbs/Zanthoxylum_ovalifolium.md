# Zanthoxylum ovalifolium

[TOC]

**Zanthoxylum ovalifolium** is an erect shrub or small tree growing up to 8 metres tall. An essential oil is obtained from the fruit.

## Chemical Composition
It contains various  phytochemical  constituents like  alkaloids, sterols,  phenolics,  lignins,  coumarins,  terpenoids, flavonoids and their  glycosides  and  benzenoids etc.
