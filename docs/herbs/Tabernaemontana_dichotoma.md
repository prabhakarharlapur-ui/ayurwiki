# Tabernaemontana dichotoma

[TOC]

![*Tabernaemontana dichotoma*](../images/Tabernaemontana_dichotoma_Sesse_and_Moc._(AM_AK296658-1).jpg)
**Tabernaemontana dichotoma** is a shrub or a tree usually growing from 2 - 12 metres tall, exceptionally to 20 metres. The bole can be 15 - 30cm in diameter. The plant is sometimes harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine.
## Uses
Ulcers, Fistula, Ripen boils, Carbuncles, Centipede bites, Snake bites, Toothache.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
It contains Monoterpenoid indole alkaloid, namely (3R,7S,14R,19S,20R)‐19 hydroxypseudovincadifformine.
