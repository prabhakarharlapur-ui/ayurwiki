# Lavandula stoechas

[TOC]

![](../images/Lavandula_stoechas_(flower).jpg)
**Lavandula stoechas** is an evergreen shrub that can grow up to 0.75 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and source of materials.
## Uses
Nausea, Wounds, Ulcers, Sores.

## Parts Used
Aromatic leaves, Flowers.

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings of half-ripe wood, Layering.

## How to plant/cultivate
Succeeds in almost any soil so long as it is well-drained and not too acid. Prefers a sunny position in a neutral to alkaline soil.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Dry hills, Garigue woods, Open woods, Granite soils.
