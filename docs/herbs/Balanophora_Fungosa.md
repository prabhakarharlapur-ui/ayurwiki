# Balanophora Fungosa

[TOC]

![](../images/Balanophora_fungosa_2_Madikeri_Coorg_India.JPG)
**Balanophora fungosa** is a fleshy, leafless herb that is total parasitic on the roots or rhizomes of various host species. A perennial plant growing from a tuberous rootstock, it grows up to 15cm tall.
The roots of at least one form of this plant are harvested locally for their wax, which is used for lighting purposes.
## Uses
Wound, Piles

## Parts Used
Root, Whole plant

## Chemical Composition
It contains phenolic compounds Isolariciresinol, Gallic acid, Pinoresinol, Methyl caffeate, and Epipinoresinol, Glucopyranoside were isolated from Balanophora fungosa.
