# Lilium lancifolium

[TOC]

![](../images/Fleur_exotique_1.jpg)
**Lilium lancifolium** is a Bulb up to 1.20 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine
## Uses
Heart diseases, Coughs, Coughs, Palpitations, Boils, Myopic astigmatism, Uterine neuralgia, Congestion, Nausea of pregnancy.

## Parts Used
Bulb, Flowers.
