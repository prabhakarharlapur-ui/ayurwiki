# Lansium domesticum

[TOC]

![](../images/Pokok_duku_langsat.JPG)
**Lansium domesticum**p is an evergreen tree usually growing 10 - 30 metres tall in the wild but generally only 5 - 10 metres in cultivation. The straight, cylindrical bole is irregularly fluted and has steep buttresses that can be 2 metres wide at the base; it can be unbranched for the majority of the tree's height and up to 75cm in diameter.
## Uses
Diarrhoea, Intestinal spasms, Dysentery, Malaria.
