# Carthamus tinctorius - Kust

[TOC]

![*Safflower*, *Carthamus tinctorius*](../images/Safflower.jpg)

**Carthamus tinctorius** is an annual plant native to the Mediterranean countries and cultivated in Europe and the U.S.

## Uses
Heart disease, Cholesterol, Tumours, Stomatitis, Fever, Eruptive skin complaints, Inflammation, Wound, Rheumatism

## Parts Used
Seeds, Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
The major constituent is the chalcone C-glucoside carthamin (up to 8.5%)

Safflower probably grows best in the semi-arid subtropics, but cultivars have been developed that widen its range and it is now cultivated from the temperate zone to higher elevations in the tropics
