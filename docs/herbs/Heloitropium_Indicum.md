# Heloitropium Indicum

[TOC]

![*Linaria vulgaris*](../images/Linaria_vulgaris_-_harilik_käokannus_Valingu.jpg)
**Heloitropium Indicum** is a coarse annual herb, Up to 60cm height.
## Uses
Warts, Inflammations, Tumours, Wounds, Skin ulcers, Thrush, Diarrhoea, Diabetes, Veneral diseases, Frequent excration of urine, Heat rash.

## Parts Used
Whole plant.

## Identification
### Leaf
Ovate, Obtuse, Wavy or slightly toothed, Somewhat cordate at base, Halves unequal at base

### Flower
Small, Pale blue, 2 ranked. Flowering season is September-March

### Fruit
Ovoid, 3-3.5mm long, Ribbed, 2 celled, Separating into 2 nutlets each. Fruiting season is September-March
