# Paeonia emodi

[TOC]

![**Paeonia emodi**](../images/Paeonia_emodi_fleur.jpg)
**Paeonia emodi** is a robust herbaceous plant. It has large white flowers and large deeply incised leaves, belonging to the peonies.
## Uses
Hypertension, Palpitations, Asthma, Uterine diseases, Colic, Bilious obstructions.

## Parts Used
Fruits, Leaves, Flower.

## Chemical Composition
Salicylaldehyde (85.5%), cis-myrtanal (4.9%), myrtenal (1.8%), trans-myrtanol (1.6%), and nopinone (1.4%).

## Identification
### Leaf
Compound, Lanceshaped in outline, 1-2 ft long, long-pointed leaflets or lobes up to 14 cm long

### Flower
Bisexual, Elliptic-shaped, White, 8-12 cm across, with 5-10 elliptic petals and 5 persistent outer sepals

### Fruit
Simple Fruit, Fruit is 3-4 cm, densely hairy or hairless, with seeds which are scarlet, Brown-black

## How to plant/cultivate
US Chemistry professor and peony breeder Arthur Percy Saunders made a cross between P. emodi and P. lactiflora that is now known as "White Innocence" (1947).

Lowland.
