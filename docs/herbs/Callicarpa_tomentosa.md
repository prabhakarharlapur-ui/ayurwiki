# Callicarpa tomentosa

[TOC]

![](../images/Callicarpa_tomentosa_-_Velvety_Beauty_Berry_at_Blathur_2017_(8).jpg)
**Callicarpa tomentosa** is a large shrub or small tree. It will grow up to 5 metres tall.
The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine.
## Uses
Fever, Liver complaints, Skin diseases

## How to plant/cultivate
Species in this genus generally require a sunny position or light dappled shade. Prefers a highly fertile well-drained loamy soil. Requires cross-pollination for good fruit production.
Plants in this genus are notably resistant to honey fungus.
