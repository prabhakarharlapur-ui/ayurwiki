# Lactuca virosa

[TOC]

![](../images/Lacvir1.jpg)
**Lactuca virosa** is a Annual/Biennial up to 1.80 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine.
## Uses
Insomnia, Anxiety, Neuroses, Dry coughs, Whooping cough, Rheumatic pain.

## Parts Used
Leaves.
