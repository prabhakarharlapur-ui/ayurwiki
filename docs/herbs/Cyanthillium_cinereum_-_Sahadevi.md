# Cyanthillium cinereum - Sahadevi

[TOC]

![*Sahadevi*, *Cyanthillium cinereum*](../images/Cyanthillium_cinereum_-_flower.JPG)
**Sahadevi** is an erect, branching, annual plant. It grows upto 15 - 80cm tall. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine.
## Uses
Wound healing, Renal calculi, Burning micturition, Insomnia, Skin diseases, Intestinal worms.

### Food
Cyanthillium cinereum can be used in Food. Tender leaves are cooked as vegetable.

## Parts Used
Root

## Chemical Composition
It contains  Caryophyllene oxide (16.7 %) was the major compound followed by n-hexadecanoic acid (8.9 %) and phytol etc.

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Alternate, Leaves variable, 2-8 x 1-3 cm, ovate, acute at both ends, thinly hairy below.

### Flower
Bisexual, Terminal corymbose heads, Pink, Peduncled; outer bracts minute, inner oblong, acute, cuspidate, hairy. Flowers 5-10, similar; corolla 3 mm long, glabrous, bluish-purple.

### Fruit
Achene, 1.5 mm long, Hairy, outer pappus 1 mm long, setaceous, inner 3 mm long.
