# Sauropus androgynus - Vitamin Plant

[TOC]

![**Multivitamin Plant**](../images/Rau_ngót_2.jpg)
**Sauropus androgynus** is a botanical name of Multivitamin Plant. It contains many health beneficiary factors. Hence it is called as Multivitamin Plant. It is one of the most popular herbs in South Asia, Southeast Asia, and China.
## Uses
Coughs, Lungs problems, Fever, Urinary problems, Relieve congestion, Eye infections, Erythema, Measles , Dysuria.

## Parts Used
Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
Adenosine,deoxy, methylsulphinyl‐adenosine, and uridine, two flavonol dioside, β‐D‐glucosyl, Rhamnosyl‐kaempferol, glucosyl, glucosyl‐kaempferol, and one rare flavonol trioside, glucosyl, glucosyl, rhamno‐syl‐kaempferol

## Habit
Shrub

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Ovate, Foliar Apex is Acuminate and Foliar Base is Cuneate

### Flower
Unisexual, 2-4cm long, Red, 5-20, Flowers Season is June - August

### Fruit
Simple Fruit, 7–10 mm, Mature Fruit Colour(s) is Purple, Red, White, Dehiscent Dry Fruit

## Mode of Propagation
Seeds, Cuttings.

## How to plant/cultivate
Seed - borne in abundance on the plants, they germinate readily.
Cuttings - they strike readily. Somewhat woody shoots, 20 - 30cm long are used

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Gardens, Cultivated areas.
