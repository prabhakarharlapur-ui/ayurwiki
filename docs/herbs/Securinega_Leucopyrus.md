## Identification
### Leaf
Unequal, Up to 3.5 x 1.5 cm, Elliptic, Oblong or ovate, Tip pointed, Base wedge-shaped, Stalk up to 1 cm, Sheathing at base

### Flower

### Fruit
Pods, 7.5 x 2 cm, Fruiting from February to May
