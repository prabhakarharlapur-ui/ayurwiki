# Eucalyptus globules

[TOC]

![](../images/1_Eucalyptus_globulus_juvenile.jpg)
**Eucalyptus globulus** is a large evergreen tree with a narrow crown that becomes rounded for trees growing in the open. It usually grows up to 55 metres tall, exceptionally to 70 metres. The straight, cylindrical bole can be up to 200cm in diameter.
## Uses
Cuts, Skin infections, Blocked nasal passages, Sore throat, Cold, Cough, Headache, Wounds, Sore throat

## Parts Used
Leaf, Oil.

## How to plant/cultivate
Eucalyptus globulus is a plant mainly of the warm temperate zone and the subtropics, it is occasionally cultivated at elevations up to 3,100 metres in the tropics.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Damp marshy areas, Hilly country, Moist loams.
