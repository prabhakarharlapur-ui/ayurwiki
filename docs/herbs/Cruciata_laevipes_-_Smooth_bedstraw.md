# Cruciata laevipes - Smooth bedstraw

[TOC]

![*Crosswort*, *Cruciata laevipes*](../images/Cruciata_laevipes_closeup.jpg)

**Crosswort** is a species of Flowering plant in the Rubiaceae family.

## Uses
Cancer, Epilepsy, Hysteria, Spasms, Tumors, Loss of appetite, Lung ailments, Chest ailments, Sore throats

## Parts Used
Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
Combination of mechanical and herbicide treatments may be most effective. This plant has a high tolerance to some herbicides

## Habit
Perennial herb

## Identification
### Leaf
Simple, Alternate, Leaves are whorled around the stem with three or more leaves per node along the stem

### Flower
Unisexual, 2-4cm long, White, 5-20, The white corolla has 4 lobes (rarely 3) which are pointed at the tip

### Fruit
Carpel, 7–10 mm, The rhizomatous root system allows this plant to reproduce vegetatively, With hooked hairs, One
