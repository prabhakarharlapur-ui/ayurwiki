# Premna tomentosa

[TOC]

![*Premna tomentosa*](../images/Premna-tomentosa-1-.jpg)
**Premna tomentosa** is a deciduous shrub or tree growing up to 20 metres tall. The bole is often crooked and fluted at the base, it can be up to 30cm in diameter, exceptionally to 50cm. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and source of wood.
## Uses
Dropsy, Sores, Diarrhoea, Skin irritation.

## Parts Used
Roots, Bark.
