![](../images/Bispebjerg_Kirkegård_2005-03.jpg)
**Populus nigra** is a deciduous tree that can grow up to 30.00 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a food, medicine and source of materials.

## How to plant/cultivate
A very easily grown plant, it does well in a heavy cold damp soil. Prefers a deep rich well-drained circumneutral soil, growing best in the south and east of Britain. Growth is much less on wet soils, on poor acid soils and on thin dry soils.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Moist ground in woods.
