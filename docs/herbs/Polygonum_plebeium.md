# Polygonum plebeium

[TOC]

![](../images/Polygonum_plebeium_(7202564686).jpg)
**Polygonum plebeium** is a much-branched, prostrate annual plant with stems 4 - 45cm long. The plant is harvested from the wild for local use as a food and medicine.
## Uses
Bowel complaints.

## Parts Used
Leaves, stem, leaves, Root.

## Chemical Composition
It contais Alkaloids, Glycosides, Flavonoids, Volatile oils, tannins, resins, coumarins, and lignans, have also been isolated from Polygoneae species etc.
