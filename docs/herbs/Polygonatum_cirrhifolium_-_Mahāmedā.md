# Polygonatum cirrhifolium - Mahāmedā

[TOC]

![*Mahāmedā*, *Polygonatum cirrhifolium*](../images/Polygonatum_cirrhifolium_0zz.jpg)
**Mahāmedā** consists of dried rhizome and root of Polygonatum cirrhifolium Royle. It is a herb found in the temperate Himalayas.
## Uses
Loss of vigour, Pain in the kidneys, Pain in the hips, Swelling, Fullness in the abdominal region, Bone joints, Skin eruptions, Cough.

## Parts Used
Leaves.

## Chemical Composition
Glucose, Sucrose
