# Grewia tenax

[TOC]

![](../images/Grewia_tenax_flower_2.jpg)
**Grewia tenax** is a multi stemmed shrub up to 2m in height, bark smooth, grey, very fibrous so that twigs are hard to break.
## Uses
Hair vermins, Colds, Chest complaints, Typhoid.

## Parts Used

## Identification
### Leaf
Alternate, Circuler in outline, 1.5-4cm in diameter, Margins toothed and tri nerved at the base

### Flower
Solitary, 1cm Long, White, Axillary placed, sepals long and axillary placed. Flowering season is February to August

### Fruit
Orange red at maturity with 1-4 spheroid lobes, Fruiting season is February to August
