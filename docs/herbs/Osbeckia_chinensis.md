# Osbeckia chinensis

[TOC]

![](../images/Nathaniel_Wallich01.jpg)
**Osbeckia chinensis** is an erect, herbaceous perennial that sometimes forms woody stems and becomes shrub-like. It ranges in height from 20 - 100 cm tall. The plant is sometimes gathered from the wild for medicinal use.
## Uses
Watery diarrhoea, Dysentery, Excessive sputum.

## Chemical Composition
It contains sixteen compounds were isolated from. Chinensis and identified as 4-0-β-D- glucopyranosyl-3-O-methylellagic acid, Glucopyranosyl-3, Methylellagic acid.
