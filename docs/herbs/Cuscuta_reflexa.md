[TOC]

![](../images/Cuscuta_reflexa_moodillathali_flowers.jpg)
**Cuscuta reflexa** is a Perennial It is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine.
## Uses
Fevers, Body pains, Itchy skin, Jaundice, Muscle pain, Coughs.
