**Reseda luteola** is a Biennial up to 1.50 metres tall. It is harvested from the wild for local use as a medicine and source of materials.

## How to plant/cultivate
Easily grown in any well-drained neutral to slightly alkaline soil in a sunny position or in some shade if the summers are long and hot.

## Commonly seen growing in areas
Disturbed ground, Arable land, Especially on calcareous soils.
