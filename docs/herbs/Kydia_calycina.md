# Kydia calycina

[TOC]

![](../images/Khabi_(in_Manipuri)_(3088953295).jpg)
**Kydia calycina** is an evergreen tree growing 10 - 20 metres tall. The plant is harvested from the wild for its fibre.
## Uses
Skin diseases, Body pains.
