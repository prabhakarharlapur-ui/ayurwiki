# Upekṣā

**Upekṣā** is disregard. Upekṣā is not only feeling of disdain for a person who has fallen into vice or a feeling of indifference or superpriority towards him. it is also a self examination to find out how one would have behaved in like circumstances and also how far he is responsible for the state of the fallen one and to help him on to the right path.
