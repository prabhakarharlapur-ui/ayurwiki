## Mūrcchana Composition
| Pārada | - | - | 1 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Gṛhakanyā (Kumārī) | - | Lf. | 1/16 part |
| [Harītakī](Harītakī.md) | - | P. | 1/16 part |
| [Bibhītaka](Bibhītaka.md) | - | P. | 1/16 part |
| [Āmalakī](Āmalakī.md) | - | P. | 1/16 part |
| [Citraka](Citraka.md) | - | Rt. | 1/16 part |

## Method
Ingredients 2 to 6 are mixed with Pārada and rubbed well in [khalva](khalva.md) for three days or till mūrcchana stage Pārada is reached. then this mercury is ready for subjecting to utthāpana samskāra.
