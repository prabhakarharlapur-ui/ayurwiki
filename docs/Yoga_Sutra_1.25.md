# Yoga Sutra 1.25

> तत्र निरतिशयं सर्वज्ञबीजम् ॥२५॥
>
> tatra niratiśayaṁ sarvajña-bījam ॥25॥

1. tatra: *there (in God)*
1. niratiśayaṁ: *not exceeded by any others, limitless*
1. sarvajña: *omniscient*
1. bījam: *principle, seed*

:**In Ishwara there is the seed of limitless omniscience.**

**Attribute of Ishwara** - In Ishwara, the seed of Omniscience has alreay have its utmost development which cannot be exceeded.

Ishwara is onmi-potent, he knows everything and controls everything, because he is the creator, nurturer, and destroyer of this universe, in which there are thousands of solar systems sustaining upon his wish. He can create and destroy many such systems within a nanosecond. But the mankind from many hundreds of years trying to solve and understand his creation, yet unable to get it completely, every day, every year, new things are being discovered even after thousands of years.
