# Ayurveda Unravelled

[TOC]

**Ayurveda Unravelled** is a book written by *Sharadini Dahanukar* and *Urmila Thatte* and published by National Book Trust of India. The book explores the mysteries of *[Dosha](../../concepts/Dosha.md)-[Dhatu](../../concepts/Dhatu.md)-[Mala](../../concepts/Mala.md)* concepts which form  the basis of Ayurveda.
