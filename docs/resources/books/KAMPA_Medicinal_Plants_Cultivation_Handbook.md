# KAMPA - Medicinal Plants Cultivation Handbook (ಔಷಧಿ ಸಸ್ಯಗಳ ಕೃಷಿ ಕೈಪಿಡಿ)

**ಔಷಧಿ ಸಸ್ಯಗಳ ಕೃಷಿ ಕೈಪಿಡಿ** (Medicinal Plants Cultivation Handbook) is a comprehensive guide published by the **Karnataka Medicinal Plants Authority (KAMPA)** (ಕರ್ನಾಟಕ ರಾಜ್ಯ ಔಷಧಿ ಗಿಡಮೂಲಿಕಾ ಪ್ರಾಧಿಕಾರ), Bengaluru, under the National Medicinal Plants Board (NMPB) programme for 2023-24.

## About

This handbook provides practical cultivation guidance for 24 commercially important medicinal plants that can be grown in Karnataka and other parts of India. It covers Good Agricultural and Collection Practice (GACP) guidelines, and for each plant provides:

- Botanical identification and common names in multiple languages
- Soil and climate requirements
- Propagation methods (seeds, cuttings, rhizomes, tubers)
- Planting details, spacing, and nursery preparation
- Irrigation and weed management
- Plant protection (pests and diseases)
- Harvest timing, methods, and post-harvest processing
- Yield estimates and economics (costs and income per hectare)
- Medicinal uses
