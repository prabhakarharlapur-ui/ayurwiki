# Four Chapters on Freedom

[TOC]

**Four Chapters on Freedom** is a commentary on the [Yoga Sūtras of Patanjali](../../Yoga_Sūtras_of_Patanjali.md) by *Swami Satyananda Saraswati*. It is first published in 1976 by *Bihar School of Yoga* later republished in 2000 by *Yoga Publications Trust, Munger, Bihar*.

This is a commentary on 196 Yoga Sūtras of Patanjali, divided into four chapters as;
* Samādhi Pāda - 51 Sūtras
* Sādhana Pāda - 55 Sūtras
* Vibhūti Pāda - 56 Sūtras
* Kaivalya Pāda - 34 Sūtras

Each Sutra (verse) is written both in Sanskrit and English letters with its meaning and detailed explanation in English.
