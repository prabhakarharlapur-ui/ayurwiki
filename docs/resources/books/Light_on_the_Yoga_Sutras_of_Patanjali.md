# Light on the Yoga Sutras of Patanjali

[TOC]

**Light on the Yoga Sūtras of Patañjali** is a commentary on the [Yoga Sūtras of Patanjali](../../Yoga_Sūtras_of_Patanjali.md). It is first published in 1966 by *George Allen & Unwin* later republished by several other publishers.

This is a commentary on 196 Yoga Sūtras of Patanjali, divided into four chapters as;
* Samādhi Pāda - 51 Sūtras
* Sādhana Pāda - 55 Sūtras
* Vibhūti Pāda - 56 Sūtras
* Kaivalya Pāda - 34 Sūtras

Each Sutra (verse) is written both in Sanskrit and English letters with its meaning and detailed explanation in English.

## Author
B.K.S. Iyengar, with a note by Yehudi Menuhin
