# Everyday Ayurveda: Daily Habits That Can Change Your Life in a Day

[TOC]

**Everyday Ayurveda** is a book written by *Dr. Bhaswati Bhattacharya* and published by *Ebury Press* on Ayurveda. The book explores daily habits and home remedies that can help in everyday life. The book delves into the traditional knowledge 'of a healthy lifestyle for living a balanced, peaceful and harmonious life'.
