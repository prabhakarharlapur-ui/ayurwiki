# Asana Pranayama Mudra Bandha

[TOC]

**Asana Pranayama Mudra Bandha** is a book on Asana, [Pranayama](../../yoga/pranayama/Pranayama.md), [Mudra](Mudra.md) and [Bandha](../../Bandha.md) by **Swami Satyananda Saraswati**. It is first published in 1969 by *Bihar School of Yoga* later republished in 2002 by *Yoga Publications Trust, Munger, Bihar*.

## Author
Swami Satyananda Saraswati
