Ayurved Vikas (Hindi) is a monthly magazine being published from Dabur India Limited for the last nearly 50 years. This is the single magazine which covers every news on Ayurveda both from National and International sectors. The very objective of Ayurved Vikas is:

1. To inform Ayurvedic practitioners about new happenings in the field of Ayurveda
1. To inform the new product launches to Ayurvedic practitioners and general public
1. To create awareness about Ayurveda / Ayurvedic therapies to general public
1. To publish research articles from leading Ayurvedic practitioners

Ayurved Vikas regularly publishes research articles from Ayurvedic consultants and teaching faculty from the most reputed Ayurvedic Medical colleges like Institute of Medical Sciences (Banaras Hindu University), National Institute of Ayurveda (Jaipur), Institute of Post Graduate Training & Research, Jamnagar (Gujarat Ayurved University) and Shri Dabur Dhanwantary Ayurvedic College, Chandigarh etc.

Dr. Durga Prasad, who had many accolades apart from his MD in Ayurveda, is the editor of the magazine. Other renowned Ayurvedic scholars like Dr. S. K. Mishra, Vd. Devendra Triguna are among the editorial consultants for this valuable magazine.
