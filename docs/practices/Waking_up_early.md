# Waking up early

[TOC]

**Waking up early** is a productivity method of rising early and consistently so as to be able to accomplish more during the day. This method has been recommended since antiquity and is now recommended by a number of personal development gurus. The philosopher Aristotle said, "It is well to be up before daybreak, for such habits contribute to health, wealth, and wisdom.

**Benjamin Franklin** is quoted to have said: **"Early to bed and early to rise, makes a man healthy, wealthy, and wise"**. It is a saying that is viewed as a commonsensical proverb, which was included in "A Method of Prayer" by Mathew Henry who also listed it as a phrase "long said."

**Franklin** is also quoted as saying: **"The early morning has gold in its mouth"**, a translation of the German proverb **"Morgenstund hat Gold im Mund"**.
