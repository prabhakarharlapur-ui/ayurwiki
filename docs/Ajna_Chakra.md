# Ajna Chakra

[TOC]

**Ajna Chakra** correspondence to the pineal and pituitary glands lying in the centre of the brain, directly above the spinal column. this is the command centre, has control over all the functions of human life. The aforementioned six chakras serve as switches for turning on different parts of the brain. the awakening of each chakra is conduced to the higher centre in the brain via channels called nadis.
