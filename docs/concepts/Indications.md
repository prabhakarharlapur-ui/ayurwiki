# Indications

**Indications** as termed by [Ayurvedic Drug Manufacturers](:Category:Drug_Manufacturers.md) or in Ayurvedic texts or studies for various disorders or ailments or imbalances.

For instance, for indications like [Sarvanga Daha](Sarvanga_Daha.md), [Netra Daha](Netra_Daha.md), [Anga pada daha](Anga_pada_daha.md) usually [Bhringamalaka Taila](../Bhringamalaka_Taila.md) is prescribed.
