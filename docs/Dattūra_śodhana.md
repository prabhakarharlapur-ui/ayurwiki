## Śodhana Composition
| Dattūra bīja |  | Sd. | 1 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Gomūtra |  | - | QS |
| Gavya payas |  | - | QS |

## Method
Dattūra seeds are soaked in Gomūtra for 12 hours. Then they are washed with water and subjected to [svedana](svedana.md) in a dolā yantra containing godugdha for 3 hours. The seeds are used after removing tetsa.
