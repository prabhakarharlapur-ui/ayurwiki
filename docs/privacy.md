# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 23, 2026

Ayurwiki ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Ayurwiki mobile application and the website at [ayurwiki.org](https://ayurwiki.org). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

## Information We Collect

### GitHub Account Information

When you choose to contribute edits through our app or website, you sign in using your GitHub account via OAuth. Through this process, we receive:

- **GitHub username** (login)
- **Display name** (if set on your GitHub profile)
- **Profile picture URL**
- **OAuth access token** (for authentication only)

We do **not** request access to your repositories, email address, or any private GitHub data. We use the minimal OAuth scope (public profile only).

### Locally Stored Data

The Ayurwiki app stores the following data locally on your device:

- **Article content** — downloaded from ayurwiki.org for offline reading
- **Bookmarks** — your saved articles
- **Drafts** — your in-progress edit suggestions
- **Authentication credentials** — your GitHub OAuth token (stored in secure device storage)

This data stays on your device and is not transmitted to any server, except when you explicitly submit an edit.

### Data We Do Not Collect

- We do **not** collect analytics or usage data
- We do **not** use tracking pixels, advertising SDKs, or third-party analytics
- We do **not** collect your location, contacts, or device identifiers
- We do **not** collect your email address

## How We Use Your Information

Your GitHub profile information is used solely to:

1. **Attribute your contributions** — when you submit an edit suggestion, your GitHub username is included in the GitHub Issue so the community can credit your contribution
2. **Authenticate your session** — your OAuth token verifies your identity when submitting edits

## Third-Party Services

### GitHub

We use GitHub OAuth for authentication and GitHub Issues for contribution management. When you sign in, you interact with GitHub's services. Please review [GitHub's Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).

### Cloudflare

Our API is hosted on Cloudflare Workers. Cloudflare may process request metadata (IP address, headers) as part of standard web infrastructure. Please review [Cloudflare's Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/).

## Data Retention

- **On your device:** Data persists until you uninstall the app or log out. Logging out removes your authentication credentials.
- **On GitHub:** Submitted edit suggestions are created as GitHub Issues on our public repository. These are public and persist as part of the project's open-source record.
- **On our servers:** We do not maintain a database of user accounts or personal information. No user data is stored on our servers beyond what is transiently processed during an API request.

## Your Rights

You can at any time:

- **Log out** from the app to remove your stored credentials
- **Uninstall** the app to remove all locally stored data
- **Revoke access** by visiting [GitHub Settings > Applications](https://github.com/settings/applications) and revoking Ayurwiki's OAuth access

## Children's Privacy

Ayurwiki does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Our service relies on GitHub accounts, which require users to be at least 13 years old.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/hpnadig/ayurwiki/issues).
