# Shakti Udyog

![*Shakti Udyog*](../images/Shakti_udyog.jpeg)

Shakti Udyog, aim at catering to the excat needs of the customers in an effective manner within their planned budget. Since there incorporation in the year **2000** as a **Manufacturer and Supplier**, there firm leaves no stone untunred when it comes to delivering a top of the line gamut of Powder & Syrups, Aditya Churna, Yuraj Dantamanjan, Pankar and other such items. They are one of the trusted Manufacturer's of Pure Ayurvedic Medicines and are FDA & GMP Certified with heritage knowledge of 100 years. There ethical business practices and competitive pricing structure has won them a great deal of trust and loyalty in the Indian markets. Giving a great deal of importance to packaging, they use the most premium quality films and robust plastic boxes, so as to make sure that each item maintains its quality at all times.
