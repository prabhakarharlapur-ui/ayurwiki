# Boerhaavia

[TOC]

![*Boerhaavia*](images/Wellness-boerhaavia-l.jpg)

**Boerhaavia**, Spreading Hogweed root extract has a diuretic effect. Helps maintain the health of the urinary system. Helps maintain the health of the kidney. Help to ease the flow of urine and reduce fluid congestion.

## Use Directions:
One [capsule](capsule.md) twice daily after food.

## Who can Benefit
Those who want to maintain a healthy kidney and urinary function.
