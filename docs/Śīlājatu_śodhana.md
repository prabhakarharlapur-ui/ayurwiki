## Śodhana Composition
| Śīlājatu | - | - | 2 parts |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Hot water | - | - | 4 parts |
| Triphalā kvātha | - | Fr.Pp | 1 part |

## Method
Powder of Śīlājatu (stone) vigourously mixed with hot water so as to disengage the soluble matter, it is allowed to settle and the supernatent suspension decanted and concentrated to thick paste or solid amorphus surface. The above extracted Śīlājatu or Śīlājatu as such available in the market is subjected to further śodhana as under.
Śīlājatuis mixed with double the quantiy of water and half the quantity of Triphalā kvātha and carefully boiled to dryness.
