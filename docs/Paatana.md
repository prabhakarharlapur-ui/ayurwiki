# Paatana

[TOC]

Pātana is of three types, viz.ūrdhvapātana, adhahpātana and tiryakpātana. Details of their procedure is as below.

## Pātana Composition
| Rasa (Pārada) | - | - | 3 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Arka cūrṇa (Tāmara) | - |  | 1 part |
| Jambīra (nimbū) rasa | - | Fr. | QS |

## Method
Arka (Tāmra) cūrna is added to Pārada and grounded well in a [khalva](khalva.md) with nimbū [svarasa](svarasa.md). this is to be pasted in the lower pot of [ḍamaru yantra](ḍamaru_yantra.md) and heated for three hours. The pārada is collected from the inside of upper pot, which is kept cool, with a wet cloth pad.
