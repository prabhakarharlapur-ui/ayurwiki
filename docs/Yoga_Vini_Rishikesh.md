# Yoga Vini Rishikesh

[TOC]

Yoga Vini is a yoga institute that imparts yoga education in the Gurukul environment. It is located near [Rishikesh](Rishikesh.md) in [Uttarakhand](Uttarakhand.md). In September 2012, [Yoga Vini] founded the Yoga Vini Welfare Society in Rishikesh.

## **Introduction**
Yoga Vini is a certified yoga and Ayurveda school in Rishikesh, Uttarakhand, which offers RYT (Registered Yoga Teacher) 200 hours Hatha Yoga teacher training course. The institute has conducted yoga teacher training programs and workshops in the country and abroad since the year 2012, through these programs and workshops, it has tried to feel and experience the effects on all aspects of life (physical, mental, spiritual). goes. Its founder and head trainer Yoga Vini is not a spiritual teacher. Yogi Vinnie developed the concept of his spine (spine) after many years of practice and study of yoga, on which his yoga training is based. In this institute, people are taught to improve lifestyle through good eating, healthy environment and through rugs and pranayama.
