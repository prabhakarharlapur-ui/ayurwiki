# Mudrā

**Mudrā** means seal, mark, or gesture in Sanskrit. It can be used to describe a sealing posture of the hand, both hands, or body.  A particular mudra should be held for a minimum of 2 minutes for one to witness their yogic effect on the body.  Depending on how open the body is to their effect, it may take up to 45 minutes.
