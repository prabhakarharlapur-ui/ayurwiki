## Śodhana Composition
| Kṛṣṇasarpa Viṣa | Snake venom |  | 4 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Sarṣapa taila | Mustard oil |  | 1 part |

## Method
Sarṣapa taila is added to Kṛṣṇasarpa Viṣa and placed in sunlight. The yellowish powder when dried is collected and stored in glass bottle.
