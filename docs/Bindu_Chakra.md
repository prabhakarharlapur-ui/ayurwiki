# Bindu Chakra

[TOC]

**Bindu Chakra** is located at the back of the head where hindus keep the tuft of hair. This nectar is secreted by bindu chakra spread through out the body by Vishuddui Chakra and the body becomes radiant. This provides the energy for procreation.
