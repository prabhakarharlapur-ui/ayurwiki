## Śodhana Composition
| Rakta citraka-mūla | - | Rt. | 1 part |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Cūrṇodaka | - | - | QS |

## Method
Small pieces of citraka-mūla are soaked in lime water and thereafter washed, dried under the sun.
