**Richly Pharmaceuticals** is an Indian **Ayurvedic Company**, **pharmaceutical company** conglomerate holding company headquartered in Diamond and silk city Surat, Gujarat,  India. It was created through a restructuring of Ayursun on December 1st, 2021 and became the parent company of Ayursun and several  Ayursun subsidiaries.

## History
Richly Means can say - in a rich manner, in full measure, Extensively, Extraordinary or beyond the world.  But here is the Meaning of Richly -   Rich by Health or Mind.
**Richly** is a sub-brand of Niramay Ayurvedic Hospital which was Started on the date of September 05th, 2000 as a medical store by Mr. VSP. Today, the company has offices across the globe, including India, South Africa, the United States, and other countries in Asia, the Middle East, and Europe.

## Products
Richly has a very wide range of products, which include "Ayurveda, Herbal, Traditional Medicine, Natural, Classical, Single Herbs, Kashayam, Cattle -veterinary feed supplement, Medicine, Dietary supplement and Nutraceuticals products.
## Structure
Beside its largest subsidiary, Ayursun Richly Pharmaceuticals has several other subsidiaries in several other industries, among others:
Subsidiary - **pharmaceutical company** - Pratik Nutraceuticals, Niramay Ayurveda, **Hospital** - Niramay Ayurvedic Hospital, Niramay Ayurvedic Stores, Niramay Ayurvedic Health Center, ** University** - Pratik University and **Non-governmental organization** - Pratik Medical education and charitable trust.
